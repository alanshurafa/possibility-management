    is to learn to feel ... together

  • "I had it backwards"

    A bold declaration from Nicole Hartley Bradford, originator of the EHP Warmup Dojo:

     

    "For decades I was creating and holding spaces for families, teams and communities to come together and experience something different from Modern Culture.

     

    Magic happened. Except when it didn't. When it didn't, it was devastating.

    Unhealed wounds drove unconscious behaviours, and relational spaces were destroyed.

    Projects fell apart. Families fell apart. Friendships fell apart.

    The pain of this led me to the Conscious Feelings Work of Possibility Management.

     

     

    Now, I create and hold spaces for healing and transformation and...

    intimately connected, alive family, team and community spaces

    exist as a consequence.

     

    Collaborative co-creation is empowered like never before.

     

    It is magical."

  • Where are YOU at?

    Maybe you want some things to heal?

    What is NEXT for you?

    Applying them requires capacity

    and skill gained by practice and feedback.

    Join an EHP Warmup

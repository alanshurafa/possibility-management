2 Rapid Learnings on Strikingly

When you make the distinction between which style of Rapid Learning to apply then you have the choice.

It is like facing two doorways.

Which do you choose?

How can you determine which doorway to choose?

The way is to practice keeping both options available and testing them out.

Will you get it right the first time? No. No one ever makes their first jump...

Each time you try you learn something.

This might be a time for you to use the Pressure method on yourself: Go! Feedback! Shift! Go!

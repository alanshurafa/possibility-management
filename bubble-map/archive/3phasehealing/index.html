3 Phase Healing on Strikingly

Human beings interact with each other through the 'gameworlds' we have arbitrarily built out of nothing. Examples of 'gameworlds' include: tic-tac-toe, checkers, chess, football, the Post Office, money, banks, credit cards, bank loans, a country, an ecovillage, a nanonation, renting an apartment, driving a car, the grocery store, a religion, health insurance, the stock exchange, commodities trading, futures trading, taxes paid to government, an army, let's have a war, etc.

You support the values of any gameworld you play in.

Are you aware of the gameworlds you play in? Could you make a list of those gameworlds, please?

Are aware of the values of the gameworlds you play in? Could you make a list of those values, please?

S.P.A.R.K. 146 says: Either you consciously design each gameworld you live in or you are a pawn in someone else’s gameworld.

We define a 'Zombie' as 'anyone who is unconscious about the values of the gameworlds they are playing in'.

SYMPTOM: You may be surrounded in your daily life by Zombies. The symptoms would be that you would need to hide out and behave like the other Zombies for fear of being singled out and abused or excluded by the other Zombies.

HEALING: Ask yourself why you have arranged to do this to yourself? If you want, change your circumstances.

SYMPTOM: You may yourself be a Zombie. The symptoms would be numbness, conformity, sleep, Low Drama, unexpected rage outbursts, ongoing background survival fear.

HEALING. Read through the Zombies website and the Psychopaths website. Figure out if you are a psychopath or not. If you are not a psychopath is will be easier for you to feel enough pain about serving the values of many of the gameworlds you play in that you will stop playing in those gameworlds. People usually do not change their behavior until it hurts too much to keep doing it the old way.

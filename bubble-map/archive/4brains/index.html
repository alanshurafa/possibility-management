4 Brains on Strikingly

This experiment goes on for 4 days.

Day 1: On this day, you are to eat only green things. On this day, you are to wear only green things (put on green clothes). If you do the gummy bear experiment, on this day take a green bear out of the bag.

On that day, you are supposed to interact with people as if your green brain is your biggest brain. How does that work? How are you doing in the role of the greenbrain?

Day 2: On this day, you should eat only red things. On this day, you are to wear only red things (put on red clothes). When you do the gummy bear experiment, on this day take a red bear out of the bag.

On that day, you are to contact people as if your red brain is your biggest brain. How does that work? How are you doing in the role of the redbrain?

Day 3: On this day, you are to eat only blue things. On this day, you are to wear only blue things (put on blue clothes). When you do the gummy bear experiment, on this day take a white bear out of the bag (because blue gummy bears don't exist).

On that day, you are supposed to interact with people as if your blue brain is your biggest brain. How does that work? How are you doing in the role of the bluebrain?

Day 4: On this day, you are to eat only yellow things. On this day, you are to wear only yellow things (put on yellow clothes). When you do the gummy bear experiment, on this day take a yellow bear out of the bag.

On that day, you are supposed to interact with people as if your yellow brain is your biggest brain. How does that work? How do you feel about the role of the yellowbrain?

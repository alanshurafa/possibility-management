Miscarriage Integration

Honouring what was, reclaiming what is.

Miscarriage is not just a medical event - it is a soul event. A rite of passage. A rupture. A revelation. After my third miscarriage, I chose something different. I entered the grief fully — and within it, I found my strength. I let go of a being I was ready to welcome home, and in doing so, I welcomed myself.

​

In the carrying of life that did not continue, I found my rage. Not destructive rage - but the kind that lifted me up with dignity. The kind that told me I could trust my body, that I could walk a path outside the systems that didn’t see me.

​

This space is for you if you want to:

  • Honour your experience in a way that feels real and sacred

  • Be held in your grief without needing to explain or fix it

  • Transform pain into personal power

  • Reconnect with your body, your intuition, and your own timing

  • Explore ceremonial or embodied ways to complete the cycle

“Within the grief, there is a remembering. Within the rage, there is a return.”

​

Private sessions available online or in person.

Ceremony available by request.

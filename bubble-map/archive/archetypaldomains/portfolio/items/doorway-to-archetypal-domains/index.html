Doorway to Archetypal Domains

This maps shows a possibility of accessing archetypal domains:
The Nothing opens and holds a space of emptyness.
The everything can enter the space and bring invitations into the space.
But they will only land in the Nothing as creational energy and information
when the everything pierces the knowing and access Everything through (within?) movement, feeling, explosion or release and creation.

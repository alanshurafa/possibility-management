nothingness

The distinction between
nothingness, as the dissociated, adaptive and non-responsible (non-responsiv?), and
Nothingness, as the present and awake, contains to possibility that men do not yet know how much really happens within them when they "go into their nothing-box". Men were (and still are...) thought to suppress what really happens. It's like a white space of silence which shuts down the noise (information from all 5 bodies).

When that noise is active, even when it's covered by the "white space" there can not be any other Everything fly because when the two everythings meet it creates a clash with a painful sensation.

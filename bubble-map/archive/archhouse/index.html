  • A NEW HUMAN CHALLENGE

    Humanity has arrived at an evolutionary stage where the necessity of learning new ways to come together and collaborate can no longer be ignored. The challenges that lie at our doorstep are considerable, and demand radical personal change.

    Almost every intelligent person dreams of more connection and community in their lives. Yet most did not learn the necessary soft skills growing up for how to harmoniously and effectively share space or create and sustain Gaian Gameworlds collaboratively.

    This is part of what we will be devoting and honing experientially in the living laboratory of the Arch House, along with the Possibilitator context and core skills.

    Every aspect of modern life as we have come to know it is unregenerative (read that as 'ecocidal', meaning, a death sentence for life on planet Earth).

    The known world is in a state of radical flux. It is groundless, and baseless, because modern culture is exterminating life on Earth at the fastest possible rate.

    All known procedures are in question... commerce, education, health, government, military, religion, economics, food production, lifestyle, transportation, clothing... but what are the answers? They too are unknown.

    This means that Arch House is, by necessity, a research center, sharing the findings from our experiments. In short, we have a lot of writing to do.

    YOUR SPECIALTY

    You do not get to choose your Specialty. Your Specialty chooses you.

    How can you tell when you Specialty has choosen you? When you wake up in the morning, and there is only one thing you want to do: Your Specialty, that is a good sign that your Specialty has found you.

    How do you get from where you are right now to having your Specialty find you? You ernestly and enthusiastically try Creation experiments. Creation experiments are very different from criticizing, reacting, tearing down, being overwhelmed, blaming your imaginary 'authority figure', ...

    Ernestly doing Creation experiments means that when you make an agreement, you complete it or you communicate. Ernestly doing Creation experiments means you experiment even if you do Not Knowing How To. A Creation experiment reflexively puts you in a Liquid State. If you avoid the Liquid State, you avoid Creation. Ernestly means you are awareness and willing to go through the Liquid State as you Create. Ernestly doing Creation experiments becomes an ecstatic possibility when you have stabilize yourself into your Adult Egostate, hence the requirement for you to enter the Possibilitator Training. Without a strong foot in your Adult Egostate, your ernestness is too easily capture by your Child Egostate wanting to complete the task for the teacher, or your Parent Egostate manipulating you and beating you up if you do not complete the task. This is not ernestness, both of those are torture.

    Archiarchy is not a survival culture. Please be well on your way to heal from your familiar survial mechanisms before applying to the Arch House & Team.

    Enthusiastically doing Creation experiments is far away from living in a pink bubble of fantasy world that you will feel nothing but Joy while doing Creation experiments with the Arch Team. Yes, there will be Joy, massive amounts of it. As much as you will feel deep Rage, immense Fear and profound Sadness. Enthuastically doing Creation experiments means it turns you on to:

    • Try things you have never tried before
    • Empower and engage a Team to support you and support each other in their own experiment
    • Create Possibility for each other along the way
    • Gifting yourself and the Team with the intelligence and resources of your Infinite Inner and Outer Resources

    The more you engage in different Creation experiments, the clearer your Specialty will design itself for you.

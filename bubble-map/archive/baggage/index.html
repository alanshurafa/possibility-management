CLOTHES

Matrix Code BAGGAGEx.10

Put on this pile also all the clothes that do not fit you anymore.
Immediately put the whole pile into a bag and take it to the old clothes container.

(There are old clothes containers in Germany. For example, the Red Cross puts them out. The Red Cross empties the containers, sorts out what is still usable and the good parts go to people who cannot afford to buy clothes).

BURN THEM

Matrix Code BAGGAGEx.15

Write down your worries for one week. Take a separate sheet of paper for each worry and begin each sentence with the words, "I worry about ..."
At the end of the week, ask yourself if you want to keep all these worries.

If your answer is yes, keep collecting. If your answer is no, ask three people if they want the worries.

If someone takes them, you are rid of them. If no one wants them, make a fire and burn the worries.

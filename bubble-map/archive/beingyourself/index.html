Being Yourself on Strikingly

Without authentic adulthood initiations, your Being remains powerless and trapped inside of your Box, exactly like a fully developed butterfly trapped inside her chrysalis, or a baby rooster trapped inside his eggshell. No wonder there is Attention Defecit Dissorder and mass shootings at schools, drugs, alcohol, marijuana, depression, suicide... What else should you do with your rage?

But modern culture has edges. Whole new domains exist beyond the thoughtware limits of modern culture.

People who discover the edges of modern culture (and also indigenous culture...) and establish their transformational bridge-building services at those edges, are called 'Edgeworkers'. Perhaps you are already an Edgeworker.

The purpose of investigating Authentic Adulthood Initiatory Processes is that unless you engage initiations whole-heartedly, your Being remains trapped inside of your Survival Strategy, possibly for the rest of your life. Then you almost never get to be yourself. All you get to be is a fearful naive Being pretending to 'be okay' inside of a phony persona designed to please other people, offend other people, dominate and control other people, or be invisible to other people. But where are located you in your own life? You are not there. So how can someone else be with you? How can you connect? How can you be intimate? The short answer is: you cannot.

An 'Archiarchal Initiation' is 'any process after which you are capable of taking more responsibility'.

Initiations build Matrix in your Being which can hold more Consciousness.

Responsibility is Consciousness in action.

In other words, Responsibility is applied Consciousness.

Consciousness is captured by the network of interwoven, or integrated, Distinctions that have already 'landed' in your Being (not in your mind).

Consciousness is everywhere, like light is everywhere during the daytime, or light is everywhere in an illuminated room. But you can only perceive the light in a physical Space when it strikes a physical object. The quality of the light you perceive is determined by the qualities of the physical object the light strikes: which colors are reflected, which colors are absorbed, the brightness, shinyness, texture, patterns, etc.

The same is true of Consciousness.

Consciousness is everywhere, but you can only perceive the consciousness in an energetic Space when it strikes a physical or energetic object. The quality of the consciousness that appears is determined by the qualities of the Matrix of Distinctions woven into the Being of the object. Rocks reflect rock consciousness. Trees reflect tree consciousness. Snails reflect snail consciousness. Cockroaches reflect cockroach consciousness. Cats reflect cat consciousness. Humans reflect human consciousness.

The difference between human consciousness and any other kind of consciousness, is that Gaia produced organic structures called 'Homo sapien sapien' which are sophisticated and complex enough that our awareness of our awareness and potentials can evolve.

Human beings' Beings can evolve to hold and make use of more and more consciousness.

The more Distinctions integrated into your Being, the more Consciousness you can catch and use. In this Evolution of Consciousness, there is no top end.

Yes, there is a beginning to the Evolutionary Path of becoming aware of yourself. The beginning is when you become aware that you are aware of awareness, aware of your potential, to some degree skillful in Causing Healing and Transformation, and so on. There is a beginning to the Path, but there is no end. This is because, although there are many Paths up the Mountain... there is no Mountain.

Your Evolutionary Path starts with your first Initiatory Process into Authentic Adulthood, 'Step 0', usually discovered by 'accident' or 'coincidence', and usually delivered to you by someone else. Step 0 usually involves shattering a worldview, or subverting part of your Box.

This shocking, painful, enlightening, scary experience is a one-way road. Once 'Humpty Dumpty' falls off the wall and shatters, he can never be put back together again, even by 'all the King's horses and all the King's men'.

You might hate that other person for their part in delivering Step 0. You might, after a while, love them for it. One day you might be called upon to provide Step 0 at exactly the perfect inopportune moment for someone else. If so, please execute it as elegantly and with as much Integrity as you can. It is possible to cause transformation without causing scars.

Unless you are internally reordered during step-by-step Initiatory Processes, unless you learn to 'navigate the transformational Liquid States', you will be left behind in the 'straightjacket' of your psycho-emotional Box, left behind playing your role as a consumable pawn or 'cannon fodder' in already obsolete gameworlds.

Something completely different from this is possible right now. (Yes, we said this already. It is still true.) The 'bad news' is that entering the new possibility can be completely blocked or sabotaged by your unconscious, uninitiated Gremlin.

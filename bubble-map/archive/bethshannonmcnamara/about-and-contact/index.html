About & Contact - Beth Shannon McNamara on Strikingly

I am a Death & Transitions Doula and Alternative Herbalist, and also work with Feelings Practitioner and Possiblity Management distinctions and energetic tools to support you in feeling, emotional healing, grieving, activating and using your voice, holding your center, and allowing your Being to be big and bright.
I invite liquid states and healing in the physical, emotional, energetic and mental bodies, with the purpose of creating a totally new culture on the planet, a culture that cares and listens deeply, and regenerates rather than extracts.
I invite you to reconnect to your anger, sadness, fear and joy as doorways for discovery and moving closer to what is real.
What do you really want?
What do you really have to say?
How does it go inside of you?
I really want to know.
The journey into your uninhibited authentic Love and Aliveness is yours to take. I hold the space and accompany you along the way. When your bodies are taken care of, there is the possibility for a fifth body to be activated, your Archetypal body where forces of the universe and Bright Principles
move through you.
I also hold an MFA from California Institute of the Arts and create transformational experimental performance art.

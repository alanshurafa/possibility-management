Creation and Learning

September 20th, Wednesday - 17h30-20h30:

-Authentic relating in the context of Possibility Management with Daphné Sarpyener

September 21st, Thursday - 15h45-17h45:

-Compost and Creation Circle (learn to compost the roadblocks into matter by creation)

Sept. 25th, Monday - 10h-12h:

-Money Cub with Gabriel H. Lechemin. Money Club is happening with Collaboration happening, with spaceholding from your fears.

Bullshit Detector on Strikingly

The human mind is capable of thinking many false thoughts and regarding them as true.

For example, “That is a sphere.” (In fact, a ‘sphere’ is a mathematical concept, ‘the set of all points equidistant from a single point in three-dimensional space.’ In reality, there only exist approximate spheres, such as the sun, or a soap bubble, or a ball-bearing, but in physical reality, all spheres have imperfections and are constantly changing.)

Or, “Here is an apple.” (In fact, there is no such thing as a single apple. Apples only grow on apple trees living in complex ecosystems with soil, air, sunlight, water, and mycelial inter-root communications.)

Or, “If there is fear, it is not love.” (In fact, conscious fear instantly supplies so many sensations such as,”Is this too close or too far? Is this mine or hers? Is this too fast or too slow? It this too early or too late?” on and on, to the degree that one could say, “If there is no conscious fear, love is not possible.”)

The thing that you think that is not true, or not realistic, or self destructive, is 'Bullshit'.

The reason for thinking Bullshit is that you have a Hidden Competing Commitment.

A Hidden Competing Commitment means you think that you are committed to one outcome, but beneath your ordinary level of awareness, you are truly committed to a different outcome.

How can you detect your true commitment? By looking at the results you create.

Results don't lie.

What you creat is what you want to create. We live in a Responsible Universe. It works like that. What you have is what you want.

You think it should work a different way?

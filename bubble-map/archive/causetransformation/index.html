Cause Transformation on Strikingly

It is an error to think that the people who come to a transformational event are looking for inspiration. These people are already fully inspired. They are so inspired they came to the event.

People do not come to a transformational work space to be entertained. They have a phone. They already have more than enough of superficial entertainment. Their life time is precious to them. They came here fully charged up and ready to work together with others.

You don’t need to introduce everybody to everybody. People arrive with the question, "Where is my clan? Who has the same calling as me? Where are my true sisters and brothers in this work? I have been missing being with them for so long. Where are they?"

People come to give away their life-long Treasure. That is their reward, that their Treasure can be given away and usefully used. They don't come to get something, but to give everything.

Putting participants in home groups is mostly a waste of time and energy because people in the group are selected at random and therefore come from different modalities. The Permaculture Designers are not interested in talking with the Homeschooling People or the Embodiment Dance People. They want to talk with the other Edgeworker Permaculture Designers and push to work, recontextualize it to more closely match with Reality and Responsibility.

You can trust the people who come to a transformational work space to do what they need to do. They only need to quickly find the other Edgeworkers in their domain so they can contribute what they bring. They are dying to contribute in a way that is well received. They have waited their whole lives for this opportunity to do their part, to creatively and effectively collaborate to bring their contribution to life.

People are interested in who is interested in joining their ‘group’.

If the organizers try to force people to be nice and conform to their pre-designed program, the participants are forced to betray themselves, to betray their purpose for coming. They will hate the designers and hate the program.

The entire event is about disruption. Change comes from disrupting patterns inside of people, between people, between people and gameworlds, and between gameworlds. These are your four objectives. Only when an old pattern is disrupted or abandonned can a new pattern emerge. Hurray for the Phoenix Process.

Make a semipermeable energetic membrane around the space with very clear requirements to enter. Establish a resilient and flexible container to expand and reshape itself to contain what the participants need. Do not let anyone take over. It is their fear trying to make the space safe for their comfort zone. It is their Gremlin. This space starts at Adulthood. Gremlins are on a leash. Run the space with a lightning fast razor sharp Sword of Clarity, but not to control, rather to be precise and to cut through Bullshit and open up new spaces in the walls of familiar assumptions and conclusions.

Participants are coming to meet the people they are supposed to meet so that their collaborative creative forces wake up and come into play. Trying to orchestrate the event, trying to package the event or run the event as if the process understandable and reasonable is shooting yourself in the foot.

Bringing in 'experts' and 'speakers' to consult the group is an insulting waste of time and loving energy. Of course 'experts from the field' should come, but they should come invisibly and sit with their cohorts for the purpose of doing creative work, not coming in with a fanfare and repeating their TED talk script. This is not an Instagram spectacle. This is creative transformation. Hello! The hell worlds lurk! The future of humanity on Earth is at stake.

Expect breakdown and chaos. Establish and navigate a resilient container to contain the polarities and paradoxes that people arrive with. Chaos is your ally in a Transformational Healing and Creation Space. Either you get chaos, or you get stiflingly insane rigidity that participants have to destroy to be free of it.

The space is being held when those within it feel safe enough to go to their edge without collapsing, and wild enough to try something untried.

People arrive as a volcano ready to erupt. If you suppress that volcano it will erupt sideways as context wars. Instead, free up the volcanos to erupt their creative forces by making a super resilient space. Chaos can be trusted. Coincidence can be allowed to prevail. Then the Bright Principles and Archetypal Lineages and E.C.C.O. can play full out.

One way to make a supersafe space is to set up a Toolshed with Emotional Healing Process (EHP) spaceholders at the ready. Announce that if anyone enters the Red Zone instruct them to go straight to the Toolshed. (there are 3 Zones (archived — not captured): Green Zone is learning and creating. Red Zone is frozen or terrified. Grey Zone is bored, probably Gremlin at work, trying to avoid being in the Red Zone. There could also be a Pink Zone of happy people hanging around in a fluffly pink fantasy world, the 'butterflies'. Try to not let the Pink Zoners take over center stage of your Transformational Circus...)

The issues seem relevant and important. Important to build a container that has flexible walls. The container - the space we are holding needs to be able to hold where the energy flows and how people show up.

People need to document their findings. Each group is a Radically Responsible Node in the Torus. Each group takes Radical Responsibility for bringing their creation to life, whatever it takes. For example, it may require creative collaboration with one or more other Nodes. This depends on accurately recorded and profusely shared documentation. Each Node needs a skilled Documenter as a member of their Node. The space needs a central documentation fanatic. Plan to write a book of the event to give the Treasure to the whole world.

There needs to be a followup plan. People want to know, "Who can I work with to accomplish and report back with new stuff afterwards?" Otherwise the Treasures will be lost.

Clicker on Strikingly

(All rights for these scenes are reserved by Warner Bros. Entertainment Inc.)

This short excerpt from the second Harry Potter film introduces Dobby the House Elf who demonstrates certain characteristics of your Gremlin plus uses his Clicker to make energetic declarations. Of course, this is from a children's story... and yet gives a clear visual demonstration that can be most helpful to get you started. It is you, not your Gremlin, who uses your Clicker, although the courage (heart-rage) to use your Clicker to go so nonlinear with such important things at the exact right (transformational) moment may come from your Gremlin. That is exactly what he is for... and he knows it... and he is waiting for you to get it... because he would much rather be doing those interesting kinds of jobs rather than merely watching porn videos, trying to avoid paying taxes, and being late to meetings.

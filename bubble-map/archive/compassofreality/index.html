Compass Of Reality on Strikingly

Without faster-than-mind Experiential Distinctions, your Mind is too slow to navigate the everychanging groundless nature of Reality. Before you realize that you wanted to set a boundary, speak up, make an invitation, create Adventure, say what you want, decide, Choose, Ask, Declare... Reality has moved on and you are left behind.

How many time have you wished you would have said this or moved into action?

You live just a few seconds, a few minutes, a few hours, a few life times (?) behind What Is. Never really taking part. Hiding behind the show that protects your from the Intensity of the Lightness of Being.

The persona that you have created tries to read the script that will keep it together. Trying to shape Reality into a predictable piece of theater, where once again, right on queue, you will say the correct line.

Your script is your set of Conclusions about the world, about yourself, and about others. It is an attempt to freeze an unpredictable, unresonable, ever moving Reality. If it finally would stay in place, maybe you could have a chance to be okay.

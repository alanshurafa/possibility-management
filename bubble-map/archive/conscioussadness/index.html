Conscious Sadness on Strikingly

The purpose of the Universe is evolution. This means that the person you are now is different than the person you were in the past. You are now a different person because you have acquired more consciousness. You have become aware of things that you did not know you were not aware of. One side-effect of this condition is the pain of remorse.

In the past, you have said things, done things, made decisions, created things (or not) that can not be undone. Looking back with more consciousness than you had allows you to be touched by what could have been possible. You can not change the past, but you can heal it.

For this experiment, ask someone to hold a listening space for you to go through a Conscious Remorse Process.

First, ask your spaceholder to read this experiment.

Sit facing one another.

Spaceholder, ask "what happened?" Create a safe listening space for the person in front of you to share what emerges. Allow the feelings, the tears, the sounds to come out.

Ask "what really touches you?"

"What could have been different?"

"What do you wish you could have created?"

Spaceholder, keep listening while staying in connection with the person in front of you. Make sure that you keep your Sword of Clarity out so that they do not start beating themselves up.

When there is nothing else to say, stay in silence for some time.

When you are ready, ask "do you need anything, like water or a blanket?"

Ask them if they want you to stay with them in silence or leave them to rest and integrate what just happened.

Conscious remorse is an expansive integration process. Feeling the pain of what could have been possible changes your shape and creates a sensitivity in you that was not there before. You might want to set up a meeting with someone to apologize or to write them a letter. In any case, do not use your remorse as a reason to beat yourself up.

After completing this experiment enter Matrix Code CONSSADx.01 in your free account at StartOver.xyz.

Conscious Theater on Strikingly

Look around you. Are you absolutely alone? Or are there people who know where you are?

Are there people around you in this environment?

Look at them slowly one at a time. What theater piece are they performing for your benefit?

What is their purpose? What effect are they trying to create in the space?

What effect are they trying to create on you personally?

To what degree is it working?

What exactly makes their theater piece effective on you?

What exactly causes their theater piece to be weak, inauthentic, unconvincing?

Are you moved? Why or why not?

Are you inspired by their theater piece? Exactly why, or why not?

What have you learned just now that could help you improve your Conscious Theater?

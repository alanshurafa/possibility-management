Conscious Will on Strikingly

Anger is an aspect of Will.

- Unconscious anger leads to Low Drama, and directly down hill into the swamp. There, I am stuck so I beat myself up because again I am stuck. This quickly turns into Gremlin self-canabalism in which I have no compassion for myself, and therefore also for anybody else. My compassion leaves or is not accessible. Forcing yourself to change is a type of will - willing change rather than allowing it to come through via compassion. Integrity can be will against self which serve the box - no evolution is possible.

Self canababilism > lack of self compassion. In gaps that are created Will is a possibility to be compassionate be with yourself and not get stuck. I notice when doing gremlin training it is easy to get stuck in this point. Possibility - to run compassion exercises alongside gremlin transformation to reduce self canabalism.

- Conscious anger > High Drama > Conscious Will > opens realms of creation

- Compassion for self (and others) opens up new possibilities.

Experiments/Practices needed to strengthen Will.  

Experiment One: Notice something and describe it in your own words. Do this with multiple items for 30 minutes at a time (perhaps on a walk). If you notice youself doing something else, return to the exercise with no judgement. Notice what happens during this time. Do this experiment regularly (suggest every day for one week) and notice the results/changes that occur. What happens to your attention? What happens to your will? What happens to other parts of yourself?

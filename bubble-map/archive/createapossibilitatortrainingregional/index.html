    Choose which Trainer Path Regional you are joining depending on their Levels of Responsibility A 'context' is a container or a framework that catches and holds certain clarities in the same way that a fishnet gathers fish. If you redesigned your fishnet with a wider or narrower mouth, with larger or finer netting, your design modifications would change the kinds of fish that you could catch.  The same is true with context.     Avoid Responsibility

    In the Trainer Path at zero-level responsibility, the Trainers meet in the psych-ward or in jail. They steal from each other and live in helplessness. The Trainers match onto characters such as the Joker.

    Child Level Responsibility

    In the Trainer Path at child-level responsibility, there are almost no distinctions.
    Trainers make no boundaries wanting to be nice to each other, they have no Sword of Clarity. They gives their center to the 'authority figures', they search each other's approval creating neediness and holding on to low drama.

    Trainers adapt to each others boxes or rebel against other boxes.

    Trainers use unconscious feelings to blame, reproach, make wrong, being right, creating low drama in all variations. They hold on to resentment, and keeping making expectations.

    Trainers are consumers and take what they need from the gameworld for their own benefit and survival and don't feed back into the space.

    Trainers in this Trainer Path live in scarcity, competition. The Trainer Path is a buffet for Gremlin-feeding frenzy.
    The Trainer Path at child-level responsibility is a slice of modern culture faking the creation of a new culture.

    Adult Level Responsibility

    The Trainer Path at adult-level responsibility request that every Trainer is equal to another.

    Trainers want to understand and to be understand. They give and receive possibilities from each other when it is asked for. They study maps and books.

    Trainer rarely share their research with others. Each Trainer is focused on making a living from their work and make their own personal gameworld flourish for their own benefit (financial and others).

    In the Adult Trainer Path, there are a number of distinctions. For example, Trainers are centered, grounded, bubbled. Trainers use their Sword of Clarity to distinguish the different Ego States and from which Ego States an offer comes from, to Yes and No, to make boundaries and distinctions. Trainers have clarity about their Gremlin, Box and Being; their Bright Principles and Shadow Principles and thrive to create Extraordinary Relationships.

    Trainers go through their emotional healing processes when needed.

    Trainer point out messes when they see them, they clean up their own messes but do not clean up messes that they didn't make.

    The Adult Trainer Path is centered on Fairness and Equality.

    High Level Responsibility

    In the Trainer Path at high-level responsibility, Trainers take more and more responsibility for applying distinctions, going through deeper, more elegant emotional healing processes, creating more Love, Collaboration, Possibility, Transformation and Clarity.

    At high-level responsibility, victimhood is still considered possible, emotional reactivity can be valid depending on the circumstances.
    Trainers stumble accidentally more and more often in archetypal spaces depending on the situation.

    Radical (Archetypal) Level Responsibility

    In the Trainer Path at radical-level responsibility, Trainers are each responsible for the level of responsibility that is being taken in the space.
    Trainers commit to their colleague’s commitment to their Archetypal Lineage. This commitment is bigger than their commitment to their own Box and Gremlin survival patterns and to your own commitment to your own Box and Gremlin survival patterns. These are Archetypal gestures. This is Love in action.
    Trainers are the space through which your Bright Principles do their work in the world. Therefore, they ask questions such as “which ‘I’ is speaking?“, “which purpose does this serve?”, “Is that a feeling or an emotion?”, “Is this a button, hook, trauma, trigger, voice or imbalance reactivity?” they notice contamination, identification or reactivity.

    Trainers share their research, celebrate their successes and provide emotional healing processes to their colleague for no financial charge.

    Trainers thrive together to consciously create Extraordinary and Archetypal spaces of Being Together, Transformation and Healing.

  • A Decision is very different from a Choice.

    A Decision happens in your mind.

    Choices occur simultaneously in all 5 Bodies, not only in your mind.

    Choices made in your mind are called 'Decisions', and they are merely intellectual.

    A Decision does not change who you are.

    A Choice changes the shape of your Being and thus who you are and your relationship with the Universe.

  • What Is Deciding?

    Often in life you are confronted with a Choice to make, but rather than Choosing (one of your 3 Powers) you try to Decide about it.

    Deciding uses the resources of your mind (your Box) and what you 'know'.

    Choosing simultaneously uses the full resources of your 5 Bodies and what you 'are'.

    Deciding this or that, here or there, yes or no, uses your Sword Of Clarity empowered by your Conscious Anger (archived — not captured).

    If you try Deciding about what you need to be Choosing about, you make crippled Choices, or no Choice at all.

  • Bad choices

    by Seth Godin

    If made freely, a choice feels like the right thing at the time.

    But we realize it was a mistake later, once the moment passes. We don’t know now what we learned in the future.

    Bad choices can be caused by:

    • Poor information
    • Shoddy analysis (including cognitive glitches and reliance on sunk costs)
    • Peer pressure
    • Manipulation
    • Hustle
    • Power imbalance
    • Focus on the short run
    • Indoctrination
    • Superstition
    • Unexamined biases

    Take a look: each of these is the product of outside forces and can be unlearned and insulated against. The good news is that we can get better at our choices.

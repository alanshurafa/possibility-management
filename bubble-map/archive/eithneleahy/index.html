Eithne Leahy on Strikingly

I am a nomadic edgeworker, writer, and aliveness celebrator orignally from the windswept plains of the United States.

I am a spaceholder using the tools of Possibility Management--I hold space for people to grow up, to break out of the prisons that keep them small, and to create the lives they really want to be living.

The world as we know it is crumbling. Patriarchy, capitalism, and imperialism have ushered in an era of ecocide, ruthless and relentless destruction of our Planet Earth, our home. Modern culture exists to imprison humans in systems of oppression, stifles the otherwise-boundless nature of human creativity, and keeps us numb to our true feelings and impulses.

I, along with thousands of Possibilitators around the world, am taking a stand for something different. I offer healing and transformational processes foin service of this new culture, because none of us can do it alone.

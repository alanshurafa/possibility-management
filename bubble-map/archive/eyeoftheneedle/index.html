Eye Of The Needle on Strikingly

Eye Of The Needle is an experiential process delivered by a Trainer in a Held and Navigated Transformational Space that offers the possibility to a prepared individual of escaping the Patriarchy.

Modern cultures around the world are Patriarchal. They are competitive, I win-you lose survival gameworlds - 'finite games' (as defined by James P. Carse in his recommended book Finite and Infinite Games) - which implement hierarchical power structures and value material possessions.

The future of humanity is emerging as Archiarchal cultures, which are the human cultures that naturally emerge after Matriarchy and Patriarchy have run their course.

Over 300 million Edgeworkers around the world (AKA Cultural Creatives) are already living in various permutations of Archan cultures. Don't get left behind playing in a stupid gameworld.

Archiarchy is an adulthood culture, centered around an economy of Authentic Adulthood Initiatory Processes. What this means is that beginning towards Archiarchy starts by escaping from the 8 Prisons.

The 8 Prisons are:

  1. Your mother's belly.
  2. Your father's control.
  3. Your parents' household.
  4. The school system.
  5. Your birth nation culture.
  6. Your birth religion culture.
  7. The Patriarchy.
  8. The Linear Life Plan.

Escaping these prisons is a formidable challenge, well undertaken on the Path of entering Adulthood. We highly recommend not trying this alone. As with mountain climbing, your chances of success multiply tremendously by engaging the processes with a Team of dedicated friends. So many Teams are possible and available...

As you can see, Escaping The Patriarchy is rather far along in the path towards Authentic Adulthood. You have plenty to do before even thinking of trying Eye Of The Needle. These are several years worth of preparations.

Nobody can do these preparations for you.

On the other hand, nobody can stop you from doing these preparations.

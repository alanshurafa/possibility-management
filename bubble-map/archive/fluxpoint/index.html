  • Flux Points

    WHAT IS A FLUX POINT?

    A Flux Point is a moment of Possibility when things can reorder in relationship with each other.

    Being able to detect and make use of Flux Points of various qualities is a crucial Possibilitator skill.

    An additional set of Possibilitator skills opens up the ability not only to detect and make use of Flux Points, but also to create Flux Points, without force or manipulation.

    This is when your Archetypal Lineage can come into full out play.

    EXAMPLES OF FLUX POINTS

    • The moment of Disillusionment.
    • The moment between sleeping and waking up.
    • The shifting of being in the Gap from one Story World to another Story World... before the new Story World solidifies.
    • The Ahah! moment of Discovering something new, that moment between seeing things one way and seeing them another way.
    • Noticing the point of Possibility that guards a freedom of movement in any of the 5 Bodies.
    • The shift between an existing Decision and a new Decision.
    • That place that is guarding against Positionality.
    • The point when a Belief surrenders to being investigated.
    • The moment an invention can emerge.
    • Experientially entering a NOW that is too small for stories, so the stories fall away because there is no longer space for them to occupy.
    • The moment the rug gets pulled out from under the Space you are identified with, when the foundation dissolves into groundlessness.
    • The moment of Identity Shift, between one Identification and the next, or between being Identified and being a Space Of Possibility, revealing the insubstantial nature of Identification.
    • The 'Triple Point' of water, the temperature at which water can be either a solid (ice), a liquid (water), or a gas (water vapor such as in a cloud or as steam).
    • The moment that the consequences of new action disrupt the previous equilibrium.

  • How To Recognize A Flux Point

    You have the ability to detect Flux Points - those moments when something different from this is possible right now. Are you afraid to use them to shift? Are you distracted? Are you more committed to keeping things the same then reinventing them? These decisions are up to you. But! Are you deciding consciously?

    Gaining Flux Point creation and navigation skills is itself a Flux Point in your life.

    You start living in the Ahah! moment when you commit to the Liquid State as your home. This is the gateway to the Present.

    It is the shift from merely Surviving to full out Living. It emerges as a commitment to Transformation rather than to Doing or Having.

    Becoming a Flux Point Agent is a sacrifice of comfort, control, manipulation, and the illusion called security. A Possibilitator makes this sacrifice to do the job of a Possibilitator, for the benefit of all Beings everywhere. This is 'big picture' relationship to what is, and to what is happening.

    You put your weapons of being smart, charming, knowledgeable, sexy, fast, etc. to hold the vulnerable kind of space where there is enough edge for the Flux Points to induce change. Such a Space is saturated in Clarity and Love. Your agency comes from staying connected with people, with Gaia, with your Bright Principles and your Archetypal Lineage, relying on Love and Clarity to keep the Space moving.

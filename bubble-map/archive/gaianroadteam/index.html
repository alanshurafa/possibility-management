Gaian Road Team on Strikingly

This is an attempt to share some of the impact and realizations residing in me after the first eight months of being on the Gaian Road Team. Of course, attempting to share this with you approaches absurdity because I am ongoingly and even now changing through Gaian Road Team processes. Nonetheless, even this statement is part of my sharing, because, perhaps that quality more than anything else is a prerequisite for being on the Team: the willingness to be ongoingly reinvented.

After all, we are Gaia in action (we are each and all, everyone, and every living thing on Earth, Gaia in action) and if nothing else, Gaia is evolution, manifesting more and more consciousness.

And this begins my report, because evolution is what I have experienced, not only in the stunning diversity in geology, plants and animals (including insects, because insects are animals!), but also in the diversity of the human expressions of love, play, and the unconditional commitment to self-and-planetary social transformations being birthed as the emergence of next culture, Archiarchy (archived — not captured).

By allowing ourselves as Gaian Road Team members to expand beyond mere reason, and to be moved into nonlinear actions by personal desires and impulses, by surprising suggestions and proposals from others, by mere 'coincidence' and happenstance from E.C.C.O. (Earth Coincidence Control Office), as well as by the outcomes of Gaian Road Team group process, we ongoingly and still encounter the courageous fringe-genius Edgeworkers hidden in plain sight, working whole heartedly as their destiny in action, their 5-body group intelligence in action, their bright principles and archetypal lineages in action, whether they would language it this way or not.

In fact, one of the most consistent and impactful feedback messages we are receiving from so many of the people interviewed is profuse gratitude for the space of listening we give people to speak into. They exclaim, “That’s a good question!” and adamantly encourage us to continue our work, weaving connections between next-culture villagers. We somehow manage to cause them to feel more inspired, filled with unexpected energy. Over and over again they confirm and assert the value and crucial importance of the Gaian Road Team’s mission: to establish critical connections between communities of sustainable thoughtware practice, thereby strengthening the field of influence for the emergence of next culture.

One of the catalytic seed-ideas we are planting wherever we go is: it is important and possible High Level Fun for tiny to small groups of people to declare themselves as a new country, a 'sustainability zone', a nanonation, a next-culture gameworld that is distinct from all the surrounding dominant cultures but in no way isolated. Through establishing themselves as an energetic configuration of commitment emerging from a particular and agreed upon context, they establish new space in the global ethnosphere and serve as a seed crystal of next culture that others can more easily follow.

We are witnessing individuals reclaim their authority to choose what culture they live in. Suddenly they no longer need to wait in hopeless frustration for a 'critical mass' of people to download next culture thoughtware before they can live in next culture together themselves. Instead they can enjoy living in the quality of culture their heart and soul desires right now, and they never have to stop and adapt to systems and paradigms that are not sustainable even when they are surrounded by them.

Meeting so many fellows - so many men and women working together in projects, hacking reality, playing full out, stepping into not-knowing-how and bringing their vision to life for the mutual benefit of all - we receive confirmation that a bright future for humanity is indeed being created. The massive successes which the Gaian Road Team is uncovering in the farthest reaches of the world - in education evolution, 3-Phase Healing modalities, economic transformations, new meeting technologies, and ongoing adulthood and archetypal initiations - are simply not reported in modern culture’s evening news. But a diverse and mutually beneficial global meshwork of next-culture communities of practice is rapidly growing, joyously real, and exquisitely tangible.

Perhaps most surprising to me, personally, is the discovery that after all my decades of lone-wolf single-fighter edgework experiments bringing Possibility Management to life through Expand The Box trainings, Possibility Labs and the Trainer Path, through Brückendorf, through the books and S.P.A.R.K.s, through the many Possibility Teams and Women’s Circles and Men’s Circles, is that for all this time, I was not actually a lone wolf. I had just simply not yet reached out to meet and visit my friends before now.

It is a profound and transformational experience for me to discover that I have so many friends.

Grow Up Now on Strikingly

Modern culture is oriented towards the material world. This is clear regarding what modern culture values, for example, possessing material objects such as titles to land, deeds to real estate, stocks and bonds, money, jewels, gold, fancy cars and technology, etc. What is not so clear is that modern culture teaches material skills. You were taught to stand, eat, walk, talk, tie your shoes, kick a ball, use a phone, write with a pen, ride a bike, drive a car, put a charge card through a vending machine, smoke cigarettes... If you are female, perhaps you were also taught to clean toilets, mop the floor, wash and iron clothes.

Few people in modern culture are taught skills of growing grains, fruit and vegetables, preserving food, making leather, working with animals to haul loads, spinning thread and yarn from hemp or wool, weaving cloth, sewing, knitting, crocheting, carving and building with wood, smeltering metals, black-smithing, welding, basket weaving, plumbing, electrical wiring and repairs, archery, sword-making, rebuilding motors and engines, on and on. If we need something we are taught to order it from Ikea or Amazon with our Credit Card, and keep working our corporate job to pay for it. Call up a repairman to fix anything that breaks. Buy cheap plastic shit from Asia.

Modern culture has no awareness of the Nonmaterial Skills that we are not taught.

Just because you do not know about something, does not mean it does not seriously influence the quality of your life.

You know this if you ever learned about something you did not know that you did not know about.

We offer you over 650 websites, books, trainings, articles, workshops, WorkTalks, video recordings, audio recordings, dojos, inviting you to learn Nonmaterial Skills that modern culture knows nothing about. Nonmaterial Creation Skills make use of Infinite Energetic Resources, so there is no need for scarcity, competition, I win you lose Gremlin games about the most important and interesting and entertaining things in life.

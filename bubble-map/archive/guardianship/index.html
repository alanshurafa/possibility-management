Guardianship on Strikingly

A Gameworld is a field of commitment between two or more people for a certain purpose emerging from a chosen context.

You may not know that you play in gameworlds.

You probably then do not know what is the context of the gameworlds you play in.

The context of a gameworld is the level of responsibility from which is relates to the world: zero level responsibility completely leaving the possibility of accountability behind, child level responsibility - making messes and not cleaning them up, adult level responsibility - the fair level of responsibility (I am responsible but you are responsible too), high level of responsibility - what would be the most responsible thing to do?, or radical level responsibility - being responsible for the level of responsibility being taken.

If you are not aware of the level of responsibility of the gameworlds you play in - their values - then you are playing in unconscious gameworld serving shadow principles. For example, competition, win-lose games, profit, power over, status position, jealousy, greed, ownership, superiority, etc...

Unconscious gameworlds protect them by avoiding the Clarity that they are only one set of choices among an almost infinity set of choices. Players in unconscious gameworld believe that they are real - like the Post Office, goverments, banks, tax offices, pharmacies, schools, lobbists, airline companies, etc... For most people, it is inconceivable to consider living without these institutions. They are not choices, they are the reality of living in the industrial age.

Who decided that an industrial civilization is what we wanted? or what we want right now?

We don't hear that question often in schools, at top levels of governments, at the COP or even at the United Nations.

The Clarity about gameworld is devastating because you might realize that you have been duped to participate in gameworlds that have been crushing your dreams, undermining your soul, and breaking your heart. That most gameworlds you play in are exterminating Life of Earth at the fastest rate possible.

You can take back your Agency to play cavite, hold and navigate space for the gameworlds that you want to live in. But one thing that is vital for the well-being and continuation of an evolutionary gameworld is having well-trained Guardians protecting both its Integrity and its Evolution.

    RADICAL ACTION AND DROPPING BAGGAGE

    I often regard many of the 'ordinary' conditions of modern culture to be Parasites that suck out any life energy or free attention that might otherwise be used for Healing, Evolution, and other Radical Action.

    For example, think how much time, energy, and attention these Modern Culture Parasites use up:
    > Watering, mowing, fertilizing your Lawn (comparing it to the neighbors)
    > Maintaining a swimming pool or spa
    > Having a 'pet' dog that you must scold, train, take on walks, clean up after
    > Paying interest on credit card debts - definitely a parasite
    > Having to watch the News
    > Being a fan of a sports team
    > Surfing the Internet, Facebook, Instagram, etc.
    > Keeping up with low drama messages from an amorphous cloud of 'fictitious friends'
    > Having a car or house with maintenance, insurances, repairs
    > Having a watch, clocks, phone that you must ongoingly look at to 'see what time it is'

    The cure for them is step-by-step Radical Simplicity by letting go of Baggage.

How do you Make Legends?

The first Legend you need to make is the Legend that you are a Legend Maker. This this Legend you need to make for yourself.

There are different approaches to conscious Legend Making:

1. Pressure Legend - shift people into a world that they already understand using logic, facts, reason, and stories. It impresses people, for example, "I've have a degree from Columbia Law School in the city of New York, and La Sorbonne University in Paris." Or, "I finished running in the Hawaii Iron Man Marathon." Or, "I have been a Nomad without a house for eight years." Or Declaring who someone is according to the potentials you see in a 5 Body Potential Scan.

2. Vacuum Legend - invite people to take a risk, to shine. You open a Doorway for them to be curious about something new. For example, "If you would build a Bridge-House, where would you make it? Who would come there?" They shine because you reveal that they have been thinking about all this. It could also be to propose an Experiment, such as, "What if you went back to work on Monday and created a Possibility Café Circle at the lunch break?"

3. Bizarre Legend - expose people to something surprising (for example, seeing you head bash with a goat) so they must conclude that you are weird in some way. If they associate with you then they would start to be seen as also being weird which opens a new self-image for them.

4. Taking an Unreasonable Stand, for example, proposing, "Let's invest €5000 in transformational books and drop them in Café's, Free Exchange Libraries, waiting rooms in Maternity Wards, etc. so people can find them as Doorways."

5. Be a Legend Teller about other people.

6. Practice and Experiment in any Space you might be in rather than conforming to expected assumptions, conclusions, etc. In other words, navigate towards Reality even if you need to ask Dangerous Questions.

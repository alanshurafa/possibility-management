Memetic Speaking on Strikingly

NOTE: This section is mostly excerpted from Memetic Speaking Practice #5.

We are trained to use highly verbal, definition-oriented, conceptual interactions to the degree that our primary approach in human encounter spaces is from our mind. A Possibilitator strives to access additional functionalities of our 5-Body system in order to produce nonlinear possibility results, such as healing, thoughtware upgrade, authentic adulthood initiatory processes, transformation, etc. which depend on spaces with enough clarity and consistency that participants can go through Liquid States using resources beyond the mind.

How can we access and navigate the memetic nature of our encounters? You can start by making friends with the General Field of Consciousness. You can have it become your ally, even though it has aspects and qualities that no human being ever tapped into or utilized before, and it is far too vast and complex to filter through your mind.

If your mind is the governor of your work space, it is far too rigid and linear to permit the General Field of Consciousness to be used as a resource. It is possible to gain unconditional access to the memetic qualities of the General Field of Consciousness in your workspace unfiltered by the mind. This involves developing a new sensing of what is possible in the field.

Respecting and utilizing the Archetypal aspects of Memetic Speaking can bring even logistical communications extraordinarily alive.

Is the lymphatic system part of the Memetic Antennae for receiving and transmitting Memetic intimacies?

What do you come into a space with, in terms of your Memetic Intentions? If the remnants of your Survival Strategy are not fully digested, then you may come into a Space fearful, wary of being hurt, adaptive, invisible, asking whose space this is. Am I accepted here? Trying to be perceived as a 'nice' person, not as a collaborative researcher.

Imagine if 10 Memetic Collaborators come into a space in order to feed the Memetic Structure of the the Context of the Space so that the Space feeds them in return!

Your Inner Structure is made out of Memes. How can we help each other build an Inner Structure with Memes that are exotic enough to serve as a Trainer, Spaceholder or Gameworld Builder?

What is the Inner Structure of a Trainer or a Gameworld Builder?

What can we become capable of creating or navigating with an Inner Structure that is both flexible and resilient?

How can we gain access to the Memes for an Inner Structure that allows us to perceive and work with not yet visible potentials?

How to hold space for and navigate the Liquid States for Memetic evolutions or transformations that give you Agency with bringing about invisible potentials?

What Memetic Inner Structure permits a person the Hit Bottom and stay there productively?

What kind of new Memetic Tools can we build for doing new Memetic Jobs?

What are the strengths of Memetic Materials to be clear about while building new Memetic Constructs to use as Memetic Tools or Inner Constructs of new design and purpose?

How can we build the Memetic Tools for doing the Memetic Jobs that never existed before?

What do keystone Memes look like, and where do they belong in your Inner Construct?

How to detect and remove or neutralize Toxic Memes that come from psychological survival strategies, religions, corporate advertising?

How do we scan for crippling Memes so we can use the standard Memetic Surgery Tools to bring a person into their own presence and Agency?

So many valuable potentials are deeply blocked by unconsciously carried Memes.

A Liquid State is absolutely neutral, whereas the Swamp maintains some kind of position such as 'I am wrong' or 'Nobody understands me'.

What is a Memetic Liquid State if Memes land in the Being and Build Matrix?

Using the question, "Could you imagine that...?" as a Memetic Tool to help establish the skeleton of a new Memetic Construct. "What if the Memetic Construct goes like this instead...?" Using the Client's imagination to make a 'blue-print' for how to redesign the Inner Construct of their Memetics.

You can use the Imagination as a temporary glue to hold Memes together in a new pattern during a Memetic Surgery until the Memetic Construct holds together by itself through making a new kind of Memetic Sense.

It is a Memetic Breakthrough when someone says, "Whoa! I never thought of that before!"

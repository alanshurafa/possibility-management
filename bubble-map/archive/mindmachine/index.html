Mind Machine on Strikingly

Your awareness of your years spent inside the machine-works of your own Mind Machine (if you had one) has generated in you the sensitivity to detect and have wise-compassion with anyone you meet or live with or work with who installed a Mind Machine in their own energetic system and are still not aware of it yet.

NOTE: It may NOT be your job to try to inform the other person about their Mind Machine. On other hand, perhaps it is your job. You will have to experiment to find this out.

This Experiment follows on from Noticing A Mind Machine In Others.

The moment you notice the other person's Mind Machine, take note which kind you think it is is, and the evidence you see for your theory. Keep in mind that it will probably be one of the 9 Kinds of Mind Machines we have already classified over the years, but it could just as easily be that you have discovered a new kind of Mind Machine that we do not know about. (If so, please share your discovery with us... clinton at nextculture dot org.)

By seeing that in addition to facing a human being, you are also facing a Mind Machine, you automatically put yourself into a Metaconversation in which you see things from a perspective that gives you practical dimensions and possibilities for creative actions that the other person does not have. This can help you create a more productive interaction with them, perhaps more productive than they have experienced for a long time.

At the same moment, your added awareness throws you into an Unfair Conversation with the person who has the Mind Machine. The 'unfairness' is because your awareness brings you more responsibility. By having more awareness about what is going on, so you automatically receive an unfair - or imbalanced - proportion of the responsibility for how things turn out. For example, the other person may be able to take Adult Level responsibility, but you will be taking an Extraordinary Level or Archetypal Level of responsibility during the interactions. That is mathematically unfair. Nonetheless, it is the usual case for a Possibilitator. The suggestion is to get used to it...

This Experiment is to use your clarity about the workings and purpose of the other person's Mind Machine to Hold and Navigate a different kind of Space for them. This other Space begins with you being Unhookable by their Survival Strategy. This means you do not react in the same way as their Survival Strategy (and also perhaps their Gremlin) expects you to react. In fact, you do not React at all. You keep a different Purpose. Your Purpose is to bypass their Mind + Mind Machine construction altogether and connect directly between your Being and their Being. Hold a Being-To-Being connection with them, see their Being from your Being, and even though you use words to communicate, do not talk to their mind. Talk to their Being.

To talk to their Being you must speak far slower than talking with their Intellectual Body. Talk at the speed of Love rather than at the speed of Mind. Use simple words and avoid complexity, insinuation, or joking around. Energetically stand next to them, at their side, as an ally of their Being rather than as an adversary of their Mind.

Then during your Being-To-Being interactions, Bypass Their Mind Machine by ignoring it. Repeat back to them what you heard them say about what they want and what they want to do, so that they experience that you got what they said, and you are actually with them where their Being is. Proceed with the energetic attitude of: "Of course I am with you where your Being is. We can do this. We are a Team. This works just fine for me. C'mon, let's do the next thing."

IMPORTANT: Be radically consistent with this style of interaction with the Mind Machine people in your life. Even if you are talking with someone else and a Mind Machine person joins your conversation, or participates at the same meeting, switch into your Consciously Unfair mode of Being-To-Bing interaction with them. This will be magical. Your Spaceholding for them at the expense of your Gremlin's 'pleasure' at playing with the other Gremlins, will pay off big-time in terms of having a new intelligent ally at your side, and perhaps even a new friend.

If at some point one of the Mind Machine people in your Circle brings up the conversation about their condition and what to do about it, probably the best recommendation we could offer is to give them a link to this website http://mindmachine.mystrikingly.com. If they come back to you and demand, "Why didn't you tell me about this sooner?" you can say, "They said you would say this... They said to tell you that, by the time you ask me about it, you have built the Matrix to effectively do something about it."

    The kinds of contradictory things to hold inside of you both at the same place and the same time are:

    • Your Commitment and the set of Possibilities you have access to.
    • Your Stand and your Unconscious Fears plus the Unconscious Fears of others.

    • The quality and quantity of Love you Source and the quality and quantity of Love you seek from others.
    • Your Preparedness and
    • Your Vacuum of longing, authentic curiosity, vulnerable Presence, and the Defensiveness of your Client.

    CREATE PHYSICAL NECESSITY BY LEARNING TO HOLD YOUR BREATH FOR ONE MINUTE

    Matrix Code NECESSIT.01

    Your body is always working. Ever since the first cell was created from your mother's egg and your father's sperm. This will continue until you take your last breath and your heart stops beating.
    In order for your body to stay alive, its needs must be met. It needs water, food, oxygen, sleep.
    Because you normally always give your body these things, you no longer feel the necessities for them.
    This experiment goes like this:
    Take a stopwatch. Hold your breath for one minute. It's a life-threatening situation for your body.
    Notice how your other bodies are unable to calm the physical body or convince it that it is possible and okay to hold your breath for an extended period of time.
    Your body will start breathing again whether you want it to or not.
    (That's why drowned people have water in their lungs: the last thing they did was breathe in. They couldn't let it go.)
    Observe yourself in those extraordinary seconds. Write what you observed immediately in your Beep! Book.

    CREATE PHYSICAL BODY NECESSITY BY NOT MOVING YOURSELF FOR ONE HOUR

    Matrix Code NECESSIT.03

    You move all the time. Walking, sitting down, picking your nose, looking around, walking away, crossing your legs, crossing your arms.
    It's one of the needs of your physical body to move.
    If you've ever been forbidden to change your lying position after surgery, you know how painful it is not only for the physical body not to be able to move.
    For this experiment, you'll need 2 people to support you. It goes like this:
    Lie down on your back. For one hour you should not move yourself. Not a finger.

    If you have the need to change something about your position, ask your two people to do it. "Please put me on my left side. I want my head to be higher. I don't want my right arm behind my back, I want it on my right hip." And so on.

    Give the instructions without moving a muscle yourself.

    Keep explaining what you need until you are comfortable.
    Talk about your necessity as if you were able to meet them yourself.
    "Please open the window. Not so wide. A little further..."

    Matrix Code NECESSIT.08

    Suppose you feel the necessity to eat something. No one stops you, the refrigerator is full, you open the refrigerator door, take out a piece of cheese and eat it.

    The need to eat disappears.
    Suppose you feel the necessity to eat something. However, you have been tied to the chair by a burglar who is cracking your safe. Even though the fridge is full, you can't do anything about your need. Because right now there are two contradictory things here: your necessity and your physical immobility.

    Observe and describe this phenomenon over the next 10 calls (online or offline) that you are in.
    What are the two things that are there at the same time contradicting each other?

    I have an idea for an experiment in this 3cell that's fallen asleep, a way to hit a snag in space like a speeding rabbit and instantly put everyone in a new tingly state. (That's the one thing).

    Because both things are in the same place at the same time, my necessity to create something other than this is starving.

Nonbinary on Strikingly

In recent years there is a wave of tension capturing the minds and hearts of many individuals

who may be trying to escape from the capitalist patriarchal empire of modern culture.

One technique for evading unwanted inclusion is to redefine your identity.

Then, if you can bend other people to somehow recognize your self-defined identity,

you may gain a sense of 'freedom' from being classifiable.

But is that sensation real, or self delusion? Possibly even a source of Gremlin satisfaction?

You strengthen that which you oppose.

What else is there?

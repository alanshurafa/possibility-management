PM Poland 2025 on Strikingly

27-31 August 2025

You carry wounds from being in public school, ranging from being adaptive, disavowing your own authority, being terrified of being creative, and holding on to negative assessments about your value from testing and teacher's comments as voices in your head. These impressions and experiences stay in you for the rest of your life. They shape what is possible for you until you do the adult and archetypal healing and initiatory processes that change your wounds into gateways.

What most people learn in school is that they hate learning, cannot write what they think about, hate reading books, despise math, and fail on purpose because when you win, it means others must lose.

School is a test environment for learning to compete and survive the rest of your life. Being oriented towards competing to survive blocks you from finding and developing your true talents and gifts, because most of your gifts are not mentioned in the curriculum. If you love something not taught at school, they imply you are crazy or dangerous.

Public school was invented 150 years ago to prepare workers to endure life as a production worker and to prepare soldiers to obey orders from 'superiors'. To send a child to school in the 21st Century is physical, emotional, intellectual, and psychological abuse.

Each day you spent in school was one less day you had to unfold your special potentials for getting prepared for what is coming.

Through specific, strong, transformational, healing and adulthood initiatory processes, Heal From School Lab is part of igniting your adulthood path in Life. Plus, during this Lab, we practice basic and advanced skills for authentic creation, radical relating, and going into discovery together.

“The plain fact is that the planet does not need more 'successful' people. But it does desperately need more peacemakers, healers, restorers, storytellers, and lovers of every kind. It needs people who live well in their places. It needs people of moral courage willing to join the fight to make human society regenerative and humane. These qualities have little to do with success as modern culture has defined it.”

― David W. Orr

Participating in an Expand The Box Training is a requirement to join the Heal From School Lab. We highly recommend that you also participate in a Rage Club (https://rageclub.org) (online or offline) as serious preparation for engaging with this Lab.

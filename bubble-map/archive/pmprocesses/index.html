    Possibility Management processes for healing, initiation, and transformation have been empirically developed by small teams of Edgeworkers, Riftwalkers, and Possibilitators throughout Europe and the Americas since 1975.

    These processes do not originate from the traditional Western psychoanalytical context of Freud and Jung. Nor do they originate from indigenous traditions, or the Eastern enlightenment traditions, or from any religious or spiritual belief system such as the cathartic processes taught by Osho Shri Rajneesh.

    These processes use distinctions, thoughtmaps, and thoughtware from the archetypal context of radical responsibility out of which Possibility Management emerges. These are authentic adulthood initiations from archiarchy, the culture that naturally emerges after matriarchy and patriarchy have run their course. They make intelligent use of both feelings and emotions (which are radically different from each other...) in processes of cathexis. Cathexis does not imagine that if you fully express emotions they go away. Cathexis uses the intelligence and energy of feelings and emotions for healing, initiations, transformation, and adventure.

    While upgrading your Thoughtware, or being healed or initiated in Possibility Management, both Distinctions and Thoughtmaps are used .

    CONDITIONS INDICATING THAT A PROCESS COULD BE USEFUL

    We have found different causes for emotional Reactivity which can be transformed through the proper and complete use of Possibility Management Processes:

    1. A 'Button' on your Box (your psychological defense strategy).
    2. A 'Hook' sent by someone else's Gremlin to wake up your Gremlin for a Gremlin feeding frenzy.
    3. A 'Trigger Phrase' in your head that you use to open up a door for a demon to take over and eat your life energy for a while.
    4. An 'Trauma' - an unhealed / incomplete trauma being restimulated in one or more of your 5 Bodies.
    5. A 'Retrieval' is a condition in which a person has sent away a piece of their energetic body and the person is ready to retrieve and re-integrate it.
    6. A 'Banishment' is a condition in which a person is carrying something extra that is no longer needed and which is ready to be sent away, such as an energetic Block, a 'Rider', an assumption, a resentment, a story, etc.
    7. A 'Matrix Building Practice' which builds out the condition in which a person wants to create some aspect of a project but is not able to because they have not built the Matrix yet. The process or initiation leads to a new practice for that person
    8. A 'Hidden Competing Commitment' which is consistently served but which creates results that are contrary or at least very different from the hoped-for outcomes.
    9. A 'Memetic Construct' which imprisons the Client in a minuscule portion of their inner world, which may be familiar and seem 'safe' or 'controllable' but which blocks the Client from their true potential and inner spaciousness.
    10. A 'Survival Strategy' built out of behaviors that force a Client to remain in a survival / scarcity / competition relationship towards the world rather than a relationship of abundant creative collaborative living rich in love.

    Sometimes several conditions arise in combination. If something is not immediately changing by addressing one of the conditions, try looking for what else it is combined with and starting over.

    4 PARTS OF A COMPLETE PROCESS

    We have found that there are 4 distinct components in a complete process to empower people to get out of an emotional reaction and back into the present. Make sure to deliver all 4 components of each process.

    1. Upgraded Thoughtware - clarity about each distinction in a new thoughtmap.
    2. A completed adulthood and radically-responsible emotional healing process such as the initiations given in this website.
    3. Simple new practices - clearly understanding the context and delivery of new practices so that they easefully integrate into your daily life because doing things the old way is simply too painful. Doing practices... as in 'a life of practice', not just understanding practices or thinking about doing practices...
    4. Enough matrix to hold the other three - in other words, enough matrix to hold the results created by the new practices, enough matrix to hold the awareness and consciousness of the new shape of your Being, enough matrix to do the new jobs that ECCO (Earth Coincidence Control Office) will give you to match your new shape of Being.

    For example, you may understand a Distinction or a new Practice in your mind, but that will be useless unless you have built enough Matrix in your Being to be Present to the Distinction, to actually live as the newly activated Distinction, to source the Distinction in any Space.

    In other words, as you have probably already discovered and observed, it is not enough for Healing or Transformation to occur that your intellect understands something new. Change occurs through becoming something new.

    WRITING UP HEALING AND TRANSFORMATION PROCESSES

    We have been learning the long hard slow painful way that documenting Processes is a central contribution to the transformational and healing arts of Possibility Management.     Many Processes are being use that have not yet been documented. You can document new Processes. HERE is a blank Possibility Management Process Write-Up Form to use. After writing up your Process, please email it to Anne-Chloé Destremau. Thank you for collaborating to build out this gameworld.

    Every write-up should specifically answer the following questions for a complete Process Write-Up:

    1. PHYSICAL SET-UP What are the physical requirements of time and space set-up?
    2. TIME SPECIFICATIONS How long will the Process take altogether? How much time per person?
    3. FORMULATION Is the Process done by the whole group at the same time? Or in pairs? Or groups of 3 or 5 or 7?
    4. POSITION Standing? Sitting? Lying down?
    5. MATERIALS What materials are needed? e.g. tissues, buckets, towels, flipcharts, other materials for TPP Healing Processes (technopenuriaphobia)
    6. THOUGHTMAPS Which Thoughtmaps are needed to map out the territory to be explored?
    7. CONTEXT What Distinctions are needed to establish the Context of the Space in which this Process does its work?
    8. SUPPLEMENTAL LINKS Please provide links to video or audio recordings of this Process actually being delivered.
    9. BEGINNING What are the exact Scripted words to Start the Process? Having the exact Script for what the Trainer says may be crucial for successful delivery of the Process. The true Script comes from the Archetypal Source Of The Process. When the Script is delivered using these precise powerful words they will take everyone in the Space back to the Source Space from which the Process originally emerged so they can have the original experience - unedited by Box or Gremlin.
    10. NAVIGATING What are the exact Scripted words to Navigate the Process?
    11. COACHING What are the exact Scripted words to Coach the Process?
    12. WRAP-UP What are the exact Scripted words to Wrap Up the Process? Discussion questions? Are Holdings needed?

Practise & Creation Weeks - Possibility Coaching Bridgehouse on Strikingly

The six different Possibility Weeks are for

practising and creating together in the context of Radical Responsibility,

on your way towards Next Culture - Archiarchy within yourself and with others.

Discovering and shaping your Inner Structure, so that the future which you want is possible.

Depending on the practise of the week, we will dive into this territory as a team. You discover, how reading different Matrix building books together builds more Matrix, how to rapidly learn from each other's experiences and discoveries. How to practise Torus Technology in daily life within an intentional community, a team or a group of collaborators. Discover the capacity to Radically Relate to your partner, friends and family members, as well as strangers and people you don't know yet. Facing your fears, your walls, sharing a safe and radical space, where it is possible to move into another space in yourself, becoming your own Authority while relating to people. Receiving live Feedback & Coaching for rapid learning. Failing forward becomes the new reference point for your unique evolution and creation. Getting closer to becoming the Being, through which your Bright Principles do their work.

+++

To part-take in a week of research and practising together, you are required to have joined at least one ETB and 1-2 Labs

or a Rage Club and/or Fear Club.

  • As a Possibilitator, you are already holding space for Transformation and Healing or you are preparing yourself to do so.
  • As a Possibility Manager, you are practising the distinctions and maps of PM in your daily life, and you are a part of a Possibility Team or are about to join one.

We have space for up to 10-15 people.

Become a part and co-shape the space!

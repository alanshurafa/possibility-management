Possibility Acting on Strikingly

Possibility Acting is a set of skills for navigating extraordinary and archetypal spaces in your inner world and in your work.

Your acting, performance, and singing can expand to include human resources that modern culture knows nothing about.

You can consciously use your fears to nurture more intelligence and dynamism in your work, and completely relax between times.

Your presence, versatility, audience contact, communication skills, access to love (and all three worlds) can expand gracefully.

We have lost too many great performers to drugs and alcohol used for suppressing the intensity of energies and forces needed for acting beyond ordinary understanding.

You can learn how to navigate all three worlds in yourself and in your work spaces.

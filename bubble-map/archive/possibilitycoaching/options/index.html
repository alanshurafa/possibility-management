OPTIONS - Possibility Coaching on Strikingly

Feelings are for handling things. Emotions are for healing things. The problem is that emotions can get mixed together and become horrendously gripping mixed emotions, such as: jealousy, aggression, revenge, isolation, despair, greed, self-pity, inferiority, spite, melancholy, resentment, hysteria, recklessness, depression, resignation, shame, guilt, hopelessness, schadenfreude (damage joy), even psychological breakdown and burnout. Your Possibility Coach can bring you through a powerful process to Unmix Emotions. It is loud and deep. You reach back through your heart with your energetic fingers and tear the mixed emotions apart, layer after layer. By the end of the unmixing you are surrounded by piles of pure feelings which your Possibility Coach can guide you to return to their proper place in your Emotional Body. Your pure anger goes back into your bones, teeth, spine, and skull. Your pure fear goes back into your nervous system, spinal cord, and brain. Your pure sadness goes back into the liquid parts of your body, blood, lymph, soft organs, eyes, tears. Your pure joy goes back into the wall of each cell in your body so your cells can laugh again. This is revolutionary knowledge, and a transformational Possibility Coaching process.

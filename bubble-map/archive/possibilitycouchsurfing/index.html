  • How possibility couchsurfing Works

    Here is how to become a Possibility Couchsurfing Host or Journeyer

    Take a look at the Expand The Box online calendar HERE.

    Take a look at the Possibility Team online calendar HERE.

    If you can't find a weekly Possibility Team near you, then why not make your own?

    At the Possibility Team website you can download your free Possibility Team Handbook with complete instructions for starting your own weekly Possibility Team. The website also offers tons of ideas for Matrix-building experiments to do and skills to practice together. Ask your own Gremlin to do the things your Box is too scared to do. Your Gremlin will have no problem doing it. He is designed for this.

    Your information will be used for three things:

    1. To confirm your attendance at an Expand The Box training or Possibility Team.
    2. To register you as an official participant in the Possibility Couchsurfing program.
    3. To locate your position on the Possibility Couchsurfing World Map.

    After you have registered and are accepted as an official Possibility Couchsurfer...

    ...you will be added as a member to the Private Possibility Couchsurfing Facebook Group.

    You will also be emailed a link to:

    1. The Possibility Couchsurfers WORLD MAP
    2. The Possibility Couchsurfers CONTACT INFO SPREADSHEET.

    Before your Journey, personally connect with potential Possibility Couchsurfing HOSTS.

    1. Tell each HOST what your conscious intention is for your travel. Write it down first in your Beep! Book.

    2. Tell your HOST where you are in your Evolutionary Journey. For example, you could tell them what you are passionate about, what your Possibility Management experience has been like, what experiments or practices you are engaging in now. If they are interested you could also tell them how many Matrix Points you have earned in the StartOver.xyz game, how many Possibility Labs you have done, what your favorite study books are. Ask your HOST about their Evolutionary Journey.

    3. Make interesting exchange offers so that your HOST will feel rewarded by your visit. Ask what you can do for them before you arrive or while you are there.

    Suggestions for during and after your Journey:

    • Upload questions, discoveries, and exciting photos with enticing captions to the private Possibility Couchsurfing Facebook Group Page.
    • Write touching articles and publish them at medium.com and your other favorite platforms.
    • Make and upload lively video blogs.
    • Tell legend-making stories about what you have learned and discovered during your Possibility Couchsurfing adventure.

    We are each on a Journey. Sometimes our Paths cross.

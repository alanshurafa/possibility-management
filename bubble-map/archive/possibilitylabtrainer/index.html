It is impossible to deliver a Possibility Lab. This is why it is so incredible. Who do you need to be to cause and navigate multi-person radical transformation Guerrilla street Theatre for 5 days in a row? Not only that - how can you be that in flowing Collaboration with another Trainer who is either more prepared or less prepared and never the same prepared as you are with Elegance and Fierceness and Playfulness?

What is your true relationship with the Death and Resurrection show?

How can you see the invisible potential in each moment with each person with one leg in their condition and another leg in the potential condition being a bridge for the potential to become actual all day and all night for 5 days for 25 people and not exhaust your own deep batteries?

The answer is you become something that is not human.

Human being cannot do this. Human being require that things be fair, things be understandable, things be reasonable, and if you were to be radically honest, human being need to be things to be comfortable. None of which are ingredients Archetypal Transformational space navigation in a Possibility Lab.

The procedure for becoming not human in the particular kind of way that gives you the readiness needed to deliver a Lab are spelled out in:

- 450 websites in the StartOver.xyz game and in the books Building Love That Lasts and Directing The Power of Conscious Feelings, and 100 Possibility Coaching demonstration videos and 50 books and 100 films at the Go Explore website and 100 podcasts at Next Culture Radio. What is your real question? Do you think somebody is going to redesign you into a Lab Trainer for you? It brings us back to the first question.

In a way the only way, to become the inhuman thing that can deliver Possibility Lab is to already have completed every single one of your human desire for success, pleasure, adventure, and wonderfulness because if a human desire is left incomplete it will forever resurface its head in the middle of the most inopportune moment and demand to be fulfilled. Have you been to the top of Kilimanjaro yet? Did you experience true Love yet? Have you watch True Lies yet? Have you eaten enough of Clinton’s World’s Best Brownies? Have you had the best orgasm yet? Have you received enough praise and admiration and fame and money and possession yet? Have you sailed around the world and visited Kathmandu? Have you been to Bali enough times? There is no answer to these questions, except that you need answers to these questions.

There is a space called ‘I Already Have Everything That I Need’. A Possibility Lab Trainer lives in that space and not only during Labs, they live there. Day and night. End of story. If you are not living there, then you are seducable. If you smoke cigarettes, then nicotine owns you and will call on you to go smoke your next cigarette without regard for anything. This is what addiction means. It means it you will cut an emotional healing process short and not go to the full end of it and call a break so you can go smoke.

If any of your worldly desires are unfulfilled, the first step towards becoming a Lab Trainer is to go fulfil those desire. Any remaining desire will subvert your ability to be a Lab Trainer.

A Lab Trainer lives in a certain relationship to magic and skills of magic and the tools of magic and the subtle energetic sensations needed to navigate magic without which the spaces you cause and occupy remain in the ordinary domains. A fully empowered Gremlin consciously at your side with Agency to subvert any subversions is a primary necessity. Being able to lightly hold and on surf on multidimensional invitations from the demands of resisting Boxes and Gremlins of clients and simultaneously the invitations from potential itself for the evolution of consciousness is needed. In addition to that, a person can only take a step that is in front of them. How do you sense that step? A person can only go through the door that is within their reach. How do you detect or create and indicate the presence of those doorways? Practice.

Practice will be the answer to almost every question that arises about becoming capable of delivering Possibility Labs.

Where is your Practice dojo?

Who are your fellow practitioners?

Ordinary people think that things were better before they were a Lab Trainer. That part does not go away. And yet, that part can be put into perspective through distinguishing experientially the Trainer part and the Human part and doing the ordinary human part as conscious theater and doing the Trainer part as Archetypal theater. In each case, what you experience is either ordinary energy or Archetypal energy and you can grow to prefer of the taste of Archetypal energy. It leaves a hum in your 5 bodies rather than a Gremlin feeding frenzy hangover.

Projections on Strikingly

(Some notes with Christine Ploschenz.)

More than ever before (en)lightened TV screens fill you with stories defined as reality while you sit in your living room chewing gum, maybe even in the dark.

This does not only concern TV but also New Age people wanting to be enlightened so badly it can fool you. It can get extreme and out of control if you forget the intelligence of even one of your bodies, because you have 5 Bodies.

You grew up in schools that gave you a picture of reality how it was or what someone already said. What they gave you is past. Media and Schools tell stories from the past. You learned about memories, not reality.

You see episodes and series the same way a traumatized person sees episodes and series… Some people are fused together with their TV episodes because they cannot differentiate between what is real and what not.

You grew up with parallel realities, even though this is a most crucial time to distinguish between what was, what will be, and what is now.

You were shown life as a projection so you often create projections to live in.

You project your episodes on others and on circumstances which automatically creates Low Drama because although the projection is real, it is not reality.

Your projection becomes such a familiar show that it gets harder and harder to live in the objective space time reality.

Your projection is an illusion held together by Emotions from the past and from the future, not by your Feelings in the now….

Your projections are illusions telling you they are enlightened, but they shadow, just like TV produces light through careful application of shadow.

Your Experiment to look behind the shadows like in Plato's story of the man in the cave. You are being shown shadows. What will you pay to become the source?

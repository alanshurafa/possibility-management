Radical Reliance on Strikingly

This experiement requires Intention, planning and a team and it is an incrediably simple experiment. The first part is to find a spaceholder. You can also go through a vision quest on offer from other contexts. If you do choose to do that, carry your culture with you. Make your own Intention, keep your Bullshit Detector on, that tells you that there is only Bullshit or Nothing, that Stories are just stories, you can choose which one to tell. You are stepping into Adulthood and A Person of Agency and able to act as an Adult in relationship with a Radically Responsible universe.

A warning here is that most contexts do not have the distinction of Authentic Adulthood and muddle it up with becoming a child of the earth, where mother nature takes care of you. THIS IS NOT AUTHENTIC ADULTHOOD. There fore if you choose to go on a vision quest that has a relationship to responsiblity as part of its context, you can still holdspace for your own experience by keep your Sword of Clarity at all times. Also check in and set your Intentions with your Three Cell before the vision quest so that you carry your own Culture with you.

Alternatively find someone who is skilled at holdingspace for Next Culture Quests, you will find them, you simply need to start asking, the right people at the right time. You can also scan someone's Matrix and trust your impulse to reach out and start connecting and talking to a certain person. They would need to have experience in holding space for deep liquid states and transformational processes aswell as having gone through at least one multi day vision quest themself.

1. Once you have found a space holder, next find a place in nature where you will not be disturbed by other people, where you can camp for three days and three nights. Then declare the dates that you will do this experiment over.

2. The preparation begins two weeks before the process,

3. Every morning sit for 12-20minutes in silence, back straight, no sleeping and start finding the question in yourself. It will be somewhere near your hunger and necessity for this experiment. It will be simple. Every day after sitting, write a little about anything that came up for you in the process.

4. 6 days before the start, stop drinking caffinated drinks, meat and refined sugar, instead bulk up your diet with plenty of water, vegetables and pulses simple carbohydrates like potatoes, rice and oats.

5. You will need to make sure you are prepared by having the following items: sleeping bag, mat, waterproof sheet/ground cover. A little first aid kit and about 40 liters of water (that will be plenty), sunblock, insect replant, warm clothing, a shovel, toilet paper. YOU DO NOT NEED: pen, paper, books, phone snacks or electronic devices.

6. On the night before come together with your space holder and name your intention for your quest.

7. On the day that your quest begins, wake before sunrise and be at the spot that you will be at for your quest by sunrise. Have all of what you need with you.

8. Dig a hole for your toilet, set up a place to sleep.

9. Stay there for three days and three nights, drink only water, come out at sunrise on the forth morning, do not go further than the marked area.

10. You are letting all distractions fall away, stripping yourself down the raw simplicity of being a human. Alot might come up, you might feel alot, there is an intensity to simply being. Your job is to radically rely that there is a being in your humaness still there when you are not "busy", "needing to get anywhere" or distracting yourself with doing. You are also healing your TPP and radically relying on the simplicity of Being.

10. When you come out, feel your Feelings and Emotions, stay in contact with your three cell and space holder. Stay in your liquid state, of the simplicity of being human, radically rely on your impulses for authentic connection from there.

After completing this experiment, please register Matrix Code RADRELIA.15 in your free account at StartOverxyz.

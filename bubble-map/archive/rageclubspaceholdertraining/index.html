Rage Club Spaceholder Training on Strikingly

Rage Club is a doorway to Archiarchy. It is not an end destination where people learn things like “three steps for managing their anger”. By holding Rage Club, you are holding open a door for people that is the start of a new life that is more Radically Responsible because they have their voice back. Similarly, being a spaceholder for Rage Club is not an end destination. It is an evolutionary path, where you continue undergoing the initiations that you need to go through so you can be more capable of empowering the circle around you from a truly Radically Responsible context.

1. Have you removed the blocks you have to consciously using your anger? Do you have your space back, your authority back, your voice back, in a way that no one can take them away? Have you cut your energetic cords and made your boundaries with parents, teachers, partners, friends, governments, and cultures? Are you able to stand in your own clarity and deliver what you have to say with empowerment and clarity to your participants, and without being adaptive or shrinking? If not, go to Rage Club.

2. Are you on the old or new map of fear? Do you have the ability to bring your fear down to nearly 0%, or is there unconscious fear constantly running your life? Do you have the ability to ride the roller coaster of fear and use it to sense doorways that are there, tools to invent, what the next thing to say is, what wild mage moves there are for you to make? Do you love your fear? Do you hear what it has to tell you and follow it? Is your fear your ally? If not, go to Fear Club.

3. Have you been through the core initiations of an Expand the Box? Have you experienced the insanity of giving your life over to stories? Are you done believing your own stories to be true? Do you know about your Box and Gremlin such that you are not identified with them? Can you see past the Box and Gremlins of those around you? Can you invoke something else in them? Can you choose, ask, and declare so that you can source what is needed in a space? If not, go to Expand the Box.

4. Have you experienced Archiarchy and what happens when people are collaborating in a Radically Responsible context? Have you started the incredible quests that are out there for you, like to complete things with your mom and dad, to claim the jewels of your Underworld, to escape the 8 prisons, to journey to the center of the Earth, to speak with your Archetypal Lineage, to open the Pearl, and more? If not, go to Possibility Lab.

5. Have you experienced what high level anger, above 90%, feels like? Have you spoken and raged from that place? Do you get it that you will not die if you turn the taps of your anger on fully? Do you get it that your anger is a resource, that it is energy for your Archetypal lineage? If not, stellate your anger.

6. Have you experienced what high level fear, above 90%, feels like? Can you feel it in your toes and in the top of your head? Do you get it that you will not die if you feel your fear? Do you get it that your fear is a resource, that it is energy for your Archetypal lineage? If not, stellate your fear.

7. Have you become an experimenter who can source new discoveries about conscious anger? Do you know how your Box wants to limit the Rage Clubs you hold? Can you sidestep and move faster than your Box? Do you know what specifically in your lineage is trying to come through your Rage Clubs? Are you unhookable? Can you turn ordinary spaces or problem conversations into the extraordinary? Can you sense the next experiment for people? Can you speak into their Being and invoke their potential for serving Gaia? Can you rattle Boxes and catch Gremlins? Can you set context for a space so that Gremlins do not even show up? If not, go to Rage Club Spaceholder Training.

8. Is your Gremlin running your life? Are you asleep at the wheel? Are you avoiding responsibility? Are you reactive? Are you playing games with people? Is your life filled with Low Drama? Are you unable to distinguish that something else could be possible? If so, go through the latest version of Gremlin Transformation Chapter 0.

9. Are you outsourcing responsibility for expanding your Box to substances? Are you using them to numb out or to temporarily circumvent your incompetencies? Are you looking for highs? Are you avoiding feeling? Are you on brain drugs? Are you destroying the ability for Matrix to form and stabilize in your bodies? Are you poisoning yourself? Are you confusing Child or Gremlin level responsibility for the Archetypal? If so, get sober from alcohol, marijuana, hard drugs, and psychedelics. Take your authority and ability to create back from these drugs. Do not give up on your ability to source your own aliveness. Go through whatever EHPs it takes. If you are on psychotropic drugs (anxiolytics, antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers): Have a long-term, 0-pressure plan in place to become drug-free. By long-term, I mean it might take 2 years or more, but they are still persistently walking in that direction. By 0-pressure, I mean going at the pace of a brain healing from having been on psychotropic drugs.

co-creating in the domain of Possibility on Strikingly

NOTE: This logo was difficult to design. We humans carry so much Baggage around Relating. If we had shown two men or two women, the Context would have been twisted to start from the LGBTQIA+ Context and would have promoted confusion. If we had shown people interacting through any kind of device or substance, e.g. footballs, bicycles, board games, cards, alcohol, cars, skateboards, etc., it would be inferred that extra equipment in required for Relating, and it is definitely NOT. If we had shown couples, or pairs, a woman and a man, for example, gazing into each other's eyes, 'everyone would know' that Archiarchal Relating is focused on ordinary sex, and it is NOT. If we had shown only children, then it would be assumed that only children are free enough to Relate, and this is so far away from the truth. If we had shown dogs, or cats, or other animals, then it would be assumed that relating with animals is the purest kind of Relating, and this is NOT what we are saying. We chose an illustration showing a father and his son, an elder and a teenager. They are not 'doing' anything. They are not talking, not listening or speaking, not solving a problem, not making a boundary. They are just standing there, being together, Relating... in all 5 Bodies. This could be a new beginning for you.

Riftwalkers on Strikingly

This website is a skill-building environment for professional transformation agents who are called to create possibility for edgeworker-organizations.

(NOTE: This website has no connection whatsoever to the 'Riftwalker' video game.)

'Riftwalker' is a profession in 'next culture' - in Archiarchy - the culture that naturally emerges after matriarchy and patriarchy have run their course.

Due to the rapid worldwide evolution of circumstances, expert Riftwalkers are a crucial catalyst for improving humankind's chances of shifting successfully to new, more responsible, local-authority cultures.

What is so difficult about the shift? Regenerative cultures demand more awareness.

More awareness equates to more responsibility.

Becoming more aware and more responsible changes the shape of your Being.

Changing the shape of your Being means going through the Rift of not knowing who you are - at least for a while - either as an individual or an organization. During such a Liquid State it is wise (and also comforting) to have a Riftwalker at your side, someone skilled navigating Rifts, someone ready to battle the Rift demons of the Shadow Worlds who inevitably show up to feed.

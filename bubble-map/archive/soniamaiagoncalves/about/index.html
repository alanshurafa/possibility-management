About - Sónia's Site on Strikingly

I place a magnifying glass on the architecture of your inner blocks - how they operate emotionally, intellectualy, and energetically.

By tracing their root and structures, I open a path that invites your potential, authenticity, aliveness, and purpose into life.

I hold space for the becoming of what already lives within you.

With presence and intuitive questions, I guide you through inner landscapes you've long sensed but never fully entered - gently, precisely, without urgency.

This is not about self-improvement.

It is about remembrance - remembering who you are when you let go of who you've been told to be.

I carry the big picture - and a deep Love for Human Being and Earth. From this Love, other realities become possible.

Realities beyond the patriarchal gameworld upheld by modern culture.

One way forward is to gather edgeworkers and support them in delivering their non-material value into the world.

I am commited to holding space for more Beings to discover and unfold what else is possible.

I hold space for Archiarchy to thrive.

For Gaia to thrive.

For Human Beings to thrive.

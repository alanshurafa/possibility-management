Work with me - Sónia's Site on Strikingly

  • A session to meet your voice as a compass for presence.

    This is a one-on-one space where voice and feeling come together to support inner regeneration.

    Through intuitive vocal exploration, you are invited to embody your feelings, unmix emotions, and discover your real voice — not as performance, but as presence.

    By connecting with the sound that wants to emerge from within, you begin to navigate your inner landscape with greater clarity. Your voice becomes a tool to access, feel, and express what has long remained unspoken.

    Whether you are beginning your feelings work or deepening your inner navigation skills, this session offers a space to refine your awareness and reconnect with your body as a living compass.

  • A space to look closely at the architecture of what blocks you — emotionally, intellectually, and energetically.
    With presence and distinctions, we trace the roots of these patterns and open new possibilities for movement and clarity.

    You learn to recognise what is blocking you from creating the life you want to create — and to use your feelings consciously, as fuel for transformation and regeneration.
    This is not about fixing, but about regenerating from within.

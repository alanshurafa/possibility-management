Split Your Attention on Strikingly

Where you attention goes, your energy goes.

1. If you put attention on an action, such as typing a message, writing an article, creating experiments for a websites, giving an interview, holding space for an EHP or a training, ... but part of your attention is being eaten by unconscious energy vampire (see below), only the amount of energy correlated to the amount of free attention you have access to can be directed towards your action. For example, one of my biggest Gremlin Egostate contaminations in my Adult Egostate was to Worry. Massive Gremlin food that would occupy maybe 30 to 40% of my attention all the time, in the background, even when I thought I was holding space. As I continue my Adult Egostate decontamination through various Initiations, Processes and Practices, I am regaining bit-by-bit my Attention for my Adult Egostate purposes and action.

Notice where does your Attention go, and by what it is eaten up. Then do the work to reclaim your Attention.

2. I have a Feminine Attention that allows me to do about 5 things at the same time. I have noticed over the years that this form of multi-dimensional action-taking is how my Creation thrive. Taking an action in one domain gives energy to the next action. But be careful! It does not always work, sometimes it is just distraction and waste of time, if the purpose it trying to surpress a Feeling (often Fear) or the need to rest and take a nap. Also, it does not apply to every type of work. For example, if I am writing an article or a website, I shift (more or less successfully) into a more Masculine Attention, which is pointed and focused on only one thing at a time.

Your Attention has its own flow in which it thrives. Find out what it is. Consider that it is not a magic recipe, meaning the maximum use of your Attention and Energy does not always function to the same formula. Your bodies are in flux, the world is in flux, your evolution is in flux - all of those elements contribute to how your Attention flows.

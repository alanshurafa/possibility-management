Sword of Clarity on Strikingly

Your Sword of Clarity is an energetic tool.

Clarity does not appear in your mind, contrary to what we have been hammered into at school. Modern culture teaches us that if you understand and comprehend and are logical, then you have the tool you need to live life.

Actually, our research is showing us that understanding is a hindrance to life. It uses only one of your 5 bodies, and a weak one at that.

Relying on your mind to figured out Life is like thinking that Google search can save your relationship. One has actually nothing to do with the other.

Clarity is not held in your mind, it is held in your 5 Bodies and in particular, Distinctions are held in your Being.

Distinctions create Clarity. Clarity creates Possibility. 

Possibility is probably one of the most undervalued currency of our time. Possibility is the number of real options available to that you have to choose from. Possibility comes from having Clarity. Clarity comes from having Distinctions. Your Sword of Clarity is your tool for applying Distinction in Reality.

There are mainly two reasons why you can't apply Clarity and Distinctions in reality.

The first reason is that you may be trying to hold Distinctions in your mind as concepts. We learn this approach in school. Mental Distinctions prove to be dangerous because they can then be used as a weapon by your Gremlin to try to be superior or to attach others to make them inferior.

The way to have a Distinction 'sink' down from your mind into your Being is through Experientially experiencing the Distinction, in other words, to become the Distinction rather than 'know about' the Distinction.

When a Distinction integrates into your Being, your Box (Survival Strategy) reorders. You will go through a Liquid State.

You commonly feel your Box reordering as an, "Ahah!" moment. You may also feel some emotions as the 'old you' dies and a 'new you' takes form. Use your Possibility Team to give you feedback and coaching and Possibilities about Experiments you can do to more fully integrate your new Distinction.

The second reason why you cannot apply new Distinctions in reality is that you think you understand the Distinction but you have not changed your identity to include the new Distinctions in your personality. You are still trying to be the same person carrying new Distinctions. This can cause a painful confusion for you. You may have new Distinctions about your Gremlin, your Hidden Competing Commitments, your Hidden Purpose, your Shadow Principles, your Brain Splits, your Past Life Vows, but they cannot integrate because you are still trying to wear your good girl or nice boy mask.

With your new Distinctions integrated into your Being, your shape changes. The Universe and E.C.C.O. naturally interact with you differently now because your changed Matrix in your Being catches more Consciousness, and you gradually become capable of serving something greater than your own personal survival.

Due to your upgringing inside of modern culture's Standard Human Intelligenct Thoughtware (S.H.I.T.), if somehow you obtained a sword in your hands, either metallic or energetic, your first consideration would be something like, "What am I supposed to do with this? What is it for? How do I use it?"

Our automatic orientation towards the sword - towards life itself - is about having something and doing something. This is the material approach to life. But in fact, for a rich life to occur, very little from the material (archived — not captured) world is needed. So much more exists for human beings, waiting for us in the nonmaterial domains.

A main understanding about a Sword Of Clarity is that it is nonmaterial. It is energetic. If you have an energetic Sword Of Clarity in your hands, who can take it away from you? Nobody! How could they grab it? Where would they hide it? The whole idea of losing your Sword Of Clarity is ridiculous. You were born with it, like your Voice Blaster, your Want Of Declaration, your Hammer Of Assertion, your Is-Glue and Is-Glue Dissolver, your Bag Of Things, your Disk Of Nothing, and all the other 13 Tools of a Possibilitator.

You were born with it. That is one way to see this. Another way is this: You were born as it. You were born as a Sword Of Clarity.

There are two reasons you may not have realized this before now. One reason is that no one ever told you that you had a Sword Of Clarity to use in your daily life, and you did not see anyone demonstrating the use of theirs. Such a thing would be difficult to think of yourself.

The second reason is that proper use of your Sword Of Clarity requires you having already built certain amount of certain qualities of Matrix. Why don't you feed steak to babies? Because their digestive system is not developed enough yet. What babies need to eat first is a few years of their mother's breast milk. That breast milk provide the minerals and calcium to grow first baby teeth then adult teeth. The baby's gut matures. Then they can eat steak, but not before.

Only after you develop these qualities and become in some ways absurdly effective at Navigating Distinctions does your Sword Of Clarity activate in a fierce and formidable practicality, allowing you to Cause things to occur or to Transform.

Then you can live as your Sword Of Clarity. You have become your Sword of Clarity in action. Being an activated Sword Of Clarity you need little else.

In my new self image - which I still use to this very instant - is that I carry around an energetic Sword of Clarity with me. I am the Sword of Clarity. You don't find me without my Sword. End of story.

The Sword of Clarity is one of a Possibilitator's 13 Tools.

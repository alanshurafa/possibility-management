The Hierarchy on Strikingly

Start this experiment by drawing in your Beep!Book a picture of the world

with a triangle on the top of it and your name at the very top of the triangle.

If your name is John, then title this map as John'hierarchy. After this, for the next three days, wear any specific clothes to signify that you are at the top of this hierarchy.

This could be shod with sparkles on them or a cape on your shoulders. Or a crown on your head with a star on it. And include in your look a scepter. This scepter could be a hammer or a wooden spoon, or those rubber plunger things from the toilet.

After assembling your look, you just star walk everywhere with that. When you see people in these three days, any person you see, you bow to them, lightly, and then you touch them on the head with your scepter and you say, "Welcome to John's Hierarchy."

You can say something like: "Today there is no tax that you must pay", and then as "What can you do for the hierarchy today?"

If people act confused or offended, you can explain that all the life you have been participating in other people's hierarchies. And for the next three days, you decided to make your own global hierarchy with you at the top. Tell to the person: "you are lucky enough to be in the presence of the oligarch or monarch."

And then repeat: "What can you do for the hierarchy today?"

NOTE: This may sound ridiculous, however, the hierarchy is the behavior of many people in the world. People at the top of most hierarchical gameworlds act as if they have ownership of everyone in the rest of their gameworld, for example, in university, administrators, corporate managers, military, the doctors at the hospital, priests at the church, etc.

During this experiment tell people you are practicing how to behave at the top of a hierarchy just in case you get promoted someday.

Take during these three days a few selfie photographs of you blessing your slaves with your scepter and post them on social media. Explaining that you're doing a queen of the hierarchy experiment.

After posting the photos in your social media please put Matrix Code THEHIERA.02 in your free account at StartOver.xyz. In the proof, please put the link of the photos. This Experiment is worth 3 Matrix Points.

Whispers from The Sourcery

And more
I finally went to the Zoo and did the experiment from S.P.A.R.K. 054, and I realised immediately: "people are primates". I recommend you do the experiment yourself.
My first Sadness Club started last week and I am amazed at the Beauty and Power of Sadness, when the space is there for it to be witnessed. It's a real edge for me, too.
I am learning to radically rely on my team as I am preparing for an Expand the Box Training: Unfold your Being up North in Kerikeri. I love what unfolds already when I let go of doing it alone.
Apart from radically relating at The Sourcery, I am also experimenting with radically relating in intimate relationship. I am astonished at the aliveness that comes from that. My experience and quest leads to the next offering, a co-creation with a fellow Trainer from Germany.

Everything emerges from nothing
I very much sense my need to honour this time of the year, the slowing down and connecting to Earth, to the darkness, to letting things brew and germinate. So much can bubble up from there when I am connected to the Source of my Being, to that which I love creating.

I am looking forward to co-creating with you.

Thoughtware Press

To order from Thoughtware Press, click 'Order' on your book choices below. This will take you to our Google form.

Please fill in the form, and we will be in touch with you shortly.

To order from another source, such as Bookshop.org, Kobo.com, Amazon.com, or Amazon.de, simply click on the link.

If your postal address is in Europe, we recommend that you order directly with Thoughtware Press. That way, less of the price of the book goes to Amazon, and more goes directly to Thoughtware Press to support the next books.

Our Possibilitator-Distributors are located in Germany, Brazil, Poland, USA, and New Zealand.

If you want to become a local distributor, reach out to Anne-Chloé Destremau.

If you are situated in a country where we do not have our own depot, we recommend you use the Print-on-Demand service by Amazon (if available) as international shipping costs are becoming increasedly more expensive.

Some Thoughtware Press books are also available in Kindle (Amazon) or Ebook (Kobo) format.

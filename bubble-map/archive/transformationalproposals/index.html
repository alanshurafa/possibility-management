  • Your Capacity To Reinvent 'Reality'

    Becoming a Person Of Agency includes the skills associated with making Transformational Proposals.

    A Transformational Proposal is a clear authentic offer which, if adopted, establish an entirely new basis or context for a gameworld.
    Initiated Adult human beings have the capacity to create and deliver Transformational Proposals.

    TAKING CHARGE OF 'REALITY'

    Your first Transformational Proposals may be too big or too small, too simple or too complex.

    A traditional proposal opens the door to incremental change.

    A ‘Transformational Proposal’ reinvents the gameworld.

    Catalysts for reinvention include:

    • Using more profound distinctions
    • Taking greater responsibility
    • Serving something greater than yourselves
    • Shifting alliances
    • Tapping more archetypal resources
    • Changing purposes, for example, from mere survival to being on a true hero’s journey.

    Such proposals may at first sound unreasonable because they originate outside the current worldview, but become exciting when people see the benefits.

    REINVENTING REALITY

    Begins with taking ownership of your innate potential to invent.

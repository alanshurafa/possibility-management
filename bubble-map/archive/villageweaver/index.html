Village Weaver on Strikingly

One of the most common ways for a village project to fall apart is the unwillingness to confront our personal habits of separation.

This is the Village Weaver's job, to notice when someone is casting themselves out, when someone feels disconnected, or there is a conflict.

The Village Weaver sniffs out discord and moves by instinct to weave the social fabric of interdependence in the village culture.

If this is you, your village needs you to do your job with magnificence!

Villaging Bridgehouse

  • A Village is not made alone

  • Distinctions, Discoveries, Adventures

    For months what has been on my horizon has been the possibility tour coming to western MA and the Villaging Bridge House. I have discovered I don’t take time to celebrate, to feel the joy that is associated with completing something I care about, the joy associated with the result of effort I put in. I notice that there is a shame that comes up when feel and share my joy as if it’s showing off.

    As the Villaging Bridgehouse came to an end — an experiment where 11 possibilitators gathered to live in a small house in the middle of the woods in Western Massachusetts for 8 days, to empower Joseph Goldin Circle in this piece of Gaia and to deepen the research of Archan Villaging — the gold that I sit with is a distinction that entered experientially in my Being:

    Radical Responsability is taking Responsability for the level of Responsability that is being taken in a space.

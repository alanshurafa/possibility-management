US POSSIBILITY TOUR - Villaging Bridgehouse

A Night of Myths and Legends

Learn from the wisdom of our ancestors.

We’ll listen to an ancient myth about family dynamics that leave things to be desired (do you get hooked by mom/dad/ex-partners?) and how we can be with others without giving up who we are.

We’ll explore this myth for wisdom and then go through feelings-based exercises to confront how we are blocking love, intimacy, connection, and healthy relating from happening.

We’ll do a deep dive into the stories we are telling ourselves that are keeping us trapped and unable to access the possibilities we want with others.

Rage Club

Your conscious anger is your life energy and clarity.

You do not have to be a violent destroyer. Nor do you have to be a voiceless, polite people pleaser with no boundaries.

We’ll experientially explore your anger by feeling and expressing it without judgement.

In small group exercises, we’ll empower each other to shift it to a conscious purpose. Reclaim your conscious anger and your ability to set boundaries, say No!, find purpose, commit to others, have a true voice, and to do this with Love.

Introduction to Emotional Healing Processes

There is a difference between feelings, which originate in the present and are for handling things, and emotions, which are unhealed wounds that originate from the past.

If you’ve ever been stuck in an emotional reactivity that won’t go away, you’ve experienced an emotion.

This is an introduction to an energetic and feelings-based process which takes you back to the root cause of your emotion, or wound, and allows you to heal it. The purpose of this workshop is to empower you to be able to go through an Emotional Healing Process for yourself and also to teach you how to hold these for others.

“Give someone a healing process and they’ll be okay for a day. Teach people how to hold emotional healing processes for each other, and they’ll be okay for a lifetime.” – someone, once.

What Is - Happening

What needs to happen to co-create a culture where it is unbearably painful to hide and pretend, as it would be blocking the precious energy and information available through each of us?

What Is – Happening is ​a Conscious Theater initiation to witness yourself and others with a Neutral Eye, meaning, without stories of good and bad, wrongness and praise, an opportunity to be simply with What Is: with reality.

You get to play both performer and audience, giving voice to what is actually going on inside of you, externalizing and embodying your inner world, without filter, peeling back unconscious layers of conditioning, discovering parts of you that you didn’t know about and going to territories you had never been before with a radically honest troupe.

How Consciously are you performing right now? And what for? Could you get realer? The stage is yours!

  • VOICES IN YOUR HEART

    I am sitting in the car next to a colleague. I am trying to let her words land in me, but I just can’t listen. I can’t listen. I know that she wants to open a door for me. She can talk as much as she wants, the problem is that she does not know the truth – that I can’t do it, that I am actually just a fake and what she’s telling me won’t help because I am not there yet. I start to feel desperate – I am scared, isn’t it awful that everybody seems to think that I can take the next step and only I know that this is not true because I don’t tell them that I actually can’t.

    It may look like I can hold space, but that is only because they don’t know that I can’t.

    I pause.

    What on earth is going on? I look at her. This is what I’ve been telling myself my whole life. I can get all the feedback in the world, and still there is always this voice, saying: “They just don’t know that I actually can’t do it – that the truth is that I am not good enough!”

    I want to scream, but I start laughing! Holy shit! It is a voice! It is a voice in my heart! It sits deep down in my heart and I think it is true because I think my heart speaks!

    The next thing I do is tell her: “You know, I just found out that I block myself from taking that next step, because I believe in this voice that you don’t know that I can’t do it and that I am not good enough and am an imposter.” I say it again.

    Magic happens. Just like with other voices in my head, the voice becomes visible in front of me and I become able to shoot it. I take my Voice Blaster and shoot it.

    Silence. Nothing except silence.

    Since that day, the voice has not come back once. No part of myself believes that I am an imposter. NONE! I know that I can do the things I want to do. The voice only needed to be shot one single time. One single shot, and a voice that has walked with me for as long as I can remember is gone.

    Our heart has a lot to say, just like our feelings do. You might have the impression that it’s very real, it might seem so true that you cannot do anything about it, because it has been true for so long. It might seem like the things your heart says can’t just be a voice. Well, there is a difference between voices and the words of our heart. Try to listen very well and start questioning your heart’s voices. One hint is that your heart’s voice speaks through your feelings – your head won’t know what it says. There’s nothing to know. If it is, on the other hand, just a voice, it’s very likely that your heart beneath it cries from pain, waiting for you to reveal its intelligence beneath those little vampire voices.

    Start shooting your voices in your head, and as soon as there is more space in your head, start doing the same with your heart. To shoot the voices in your heart just repeat them out loud a few times, until they appear in front of you. This practice can help you distinguish between voices and something else. If you don’t know what a Voice Blaster is and want to know more, just give me a call or visit this website.

  • The Longest Walk...

    Below is a map of the longest straight walk possible on planet Earth without the need to cross the ocean or any other significant barrier.

    The walk goes from Cape Town (South Africa) to Magadan (Russia) - or the other way around.

    There is no need for planes or boats as there are bridges.

    This walk is 21,808 km, which is estimated to take an average of 4,310 hours to walk.

    This equates to 561 days walking 8 hours a day.

    Along the way, you pass through 17 countries, six time zones, 3 war zones, and every season of the year.

    Anyone coming along?

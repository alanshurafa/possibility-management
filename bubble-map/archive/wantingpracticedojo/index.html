Wanting Practice Dojo on Strikingly

Possibility Management offers initiatory processes to become adult and take Radical Responsibility for what you feel. The information and energy of your conscious Anger, Sadness, Fear and Joy are the rocket fuel for you to actually feel, what you want, to say, what you want and to create, what you want.

This needs practice.

A Dojo is a space for meeting others for a regular practice of skills.

In the "Woman, what do you want?" Practice Dojo we use each others presence and the human capacity to as questions, listen and hold space.

We practice asking the question right through all defense mechanisms, concepts, beliefs, stories into the other woman's being.

We keep asking "What do you want?" and navigating towards the feelings.

Even if you were not asked this question ever before.

It is never too late to start practicing to say, what you want.

There are no requirements (except watching the two short videos below).

Come as you are.

There are women in the Dojo, who have been exercising their skills of using their feelings consciously and holding space for others and women, who are completely new to the context of Possibility Management. We work as a team to make Learning, Evolution, Transformation and Healing happen.

You will be encouraged to use and raise your conscious Rage (archived — not captured) in this space to become access to what you want.

Your Sadness, Fear and Joy are also welcome, they hold valuable information about what is dear to you and what you want.

If you get stuck or experience emotional charge your spaceholder will accompany you in finding out, what the Emotional Healing Process (EHP) is, that is a doorway to heal the blocks from saying, what you want. You can then write down the sentence, that is this doorway in the back of your BEEP! Book and ask at the end of the Dojo, who would be spaceholder for you to go through this EHP.

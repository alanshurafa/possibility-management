What Next? on Strikingly

Specifically what is next for YOU? (...since you do not get to choose what is next for anyone else.)

Let us suppose that already along your adulthood path you have:

  • Upgraded some of your Standard Human Intelligence Thoughtware.
  • Used the resources of your 5 Bodies to escape from at least some of the 8 Prisons.
  • Accessed Experiential Reality.
  • Centered yourself into First Position.
  • Learned to go Nonlinear and Unreasonable.
  • Trained up in some of your 13 Tools.
  • Distilled your Bright Principles.
  • Provided Nonmaterial Value at a number of EHP Dojos (Emotional Healing Process).
  • Transformed your Gremlin so it is at your side serving you, and not the other way around.
  • Defused most of your Reactivity.
  • Established a working relationship with some facets of your Archetypal Lineage.
  • Learned to step out of Hierarchies and use Torus Meeting Technologies.
  • Decontaminated (archived — not captured) your Adult Ego State from Child, Parent, Gremlin, and Demon Ego States.
  • Faced into some of the coming storms.
  • Realized that alone, you are indeed screwed.

If so, then perhaps you are experiencing some form of hopelessness, despair, deep unfocused terror, desperation, or undirected outrage. (By the way, this is the state of far more people than might be admitting it right now.)

These sensations of hopelessness, despair, even boredom, are useful conditions... if you know what they are for and how to use them. They are not a mistake.

This website offers you some possibilities of how to understand and use your desperate powerlessness for Fun and Profit...

The point is, one or the other of the possibilities described here could possible turn you on at a deep level, or it could trigger a possibility in you that will turn you on at the archetypal level. In other words, at one point you may experience a tingling sensation down your spine and visions may come pouring into your heart and mind. When this happens, we encourage you to Radically Rely on it and take useful actions. Start. Just go do it.

Let's say you don't do what you are getting inspired to do. Let's say you Notice a confirming sense of what you came to Earth to do... and you do NOT do it. Let's say your mind starts thinking of Reasons you cannot do the thing your heart wants to do most. By now that should be a familiar disappointment... so familiar that you redefine this horrible sensation as 'normal'. You made it so that you do not feel 'normal' unless what you really want to do is prohibited by some external authority figure who you can blame and hate for stopping your life.

For example, let's say you are moved to have Spanish oak trees replanted all over Spain, or you are moved to protect sharks from being slaughtered so Chinese rich people can eat their dorsal fins, or you are moved to bring refugees into a completely new kind of empowering refugee camp where the Clarity about what does not work that forced them to leave their homes is used to build and inhabit regenerative-culture nanonations... and you don't do it because you feel fear about it and your mind starts finding reasons to justify the fear, such as: "It is probably against the law. Someone else will do it. The government will take care of it. I don't know how. I don't have permission or certification. I don't have the energy to do it. I have to make money to survive. What will my parents think? I am not good enough. Someone will stop me. People will think I am weird. I will fail." On and on and on...

What if the fear you are feeling is actually excitement about trying something unreasonable? What if the fear you feel is the natural wisdom-seeking experience of going to the edge of the known and implementing new possibilities even if you do not already know how to do it? Just because you have let this stop you before does not mean you have to continue the pattern and let it stop you NOW.

You are right that no one can force you, or choose for you, to go ahead and shift gameworlds in your life. No one can blame you for staying ordinary, trying to fit in to the capitalist patriarchal empire and seek success and comforts in the well-advertised 'good life'. What you do each day is a very personal decision. No one can decide for you what you do with your life... so... are you doing with your life what you really want to be doing with your life?

You will never get this time back. The days, weeks, months, years you spend trying to make enough money to pay rent to the stay-at-home 'landlords' by doing things that do not actually nurture your Being... you won't get those years back, even when you 'retire'.

If you cast away your inhibitions and take your circumstances into your own hands by making a few unexpected right-angle turns, and if you bring your kids along with you, your partner, and maybe some friends, or maybe you leave them behind... and you go out and try something extraordinary, who knows what will happen? Maybe it works. Maybe it does not work.

If you do not follow the gut-level unreasonable impulses of your calling and decide to suppress the impulses, trying to keep everything under control and 'normal', who knows what will happen? Perhaps the refugees move into your neighborhood. Perhaps there is a shooting at your kids' school. Perhaps you get cancer.

In any case you cannot know what will happen next. Clear?

If you cannot know what is going to happen whether you do nothing different or do everything different, why not spend your life doing what you came here to do?

New futures come from the people who decide, "Hell, yes! I am going to shift out of ordinary and get on track with my destiny! I think I can do it!"

New futures do NOT come from the people who decide, "Hell, no! I am not trying anything different. I am going to stay on track in the linear-life-plan and try to get ahead in the rat race! I think I can do it!"

What if it is the case that you are right whichever you choose?

What if you notice that you are still reading these wild ideas?

The part in you still reading is the part who is starving for a life that is more accurately yours.

How much longer will it take before you have participated in the old future ('Game A') long enough?

Are you ready to choose 'Game~B'?

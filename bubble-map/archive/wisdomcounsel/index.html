Wisdom Counsel - a Torus meeting technology on Strikingly

Wisdom Counsel requires a specific form of spaceholding to function. This circular meeting technology will probably not provide value in the context of modern culture, that is, in a culture that permits organizations to use hierarchical power structure, that does not distinguish Gremlin, that regards feelings as bad, that avoids distinguishing feelings from emotions, that cannot distinguish Low Drama from High Drama, that grants space to unconscious declaring (such as beliefs, projections, assumptions, expectations, prejudices, judgements) etc.

The Space in which Wisdom Counsel produces its remarkable results relies on a particular Spaceholder capable of setting the Wisdom Counsel Context. Such skills are characteristic of a Possibilitator.

This Experiment is to discover precisely what a Possibilitator is, and determine if serving your village as a Possibilitator is part of your calling.

Possibilitator is, indeed, a lifelong Path, and... you start where you are. This start is actual. A clear start comes from shifting your ordinary daily identity. What you leave behind is the self-image that blends your whole life of other people's expectations of you, your stories about yourself, complete with self-criticisms, failures and successes into a scenario that you put on like a body-glove each morning when you wake up. What you step into is the self-image of a Possibilitator.

You will know when this identity shift Experiment is successful when what previously triggered you into Reactivity no longer does so, and the energy that was used up in your daily Reactivity gets freed up to use in creating new Possibilities for other people.

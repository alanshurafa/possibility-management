THE EXPERIMENT

Gaia has included the evolution of consciousness in the human design.

This Experiment has 3 PARTS which you can do alone or with others online or offline, for example at your Possibility Team or Workshop. All parts need you to use your Beep! Book.

PART 1: Title a new page in your Beep! Book:

HOW I BLOCK MY BEING FROM SHOWING UP.

You may block your Being from showing up in the world out of fear that the worst thing that ever happened to you in your life will happen again.

1) Let your Being write for 10 minutes. It knows how it is being blocked from showing up, feeling, sensing, speaking, moving, participating, sharing, giving feedback and coaching, revealing ideas, and so on.

2) This might be painful to feel how much you block your Being. Feel the pain. Let the sounds out. Maybe even say out loud what you find to help others find this in themselves too.

3) Keep writing.

PART2: Title the next page in your Beep! Book:

HOW I BLOCK MY BEING FROM EVOLVING.

1) Let your Being write for 10 minutes.

2) Start writing without figuring out before-hand what you will write.

3)Your Being itself knows from its own natural attraction to evolution how you block your Being from evolving.

Note: In many cases, blocking your Being from evolving has to do with numbing yourself with smoking, alcohol, drugs, speeding, spending, flirting, self-hatred, mixed emotions like jealousy, feeding demons, etc. Your Box and mind are extremely creative when it comes to finding new ways to block evolution, for example, being weak, being confused, being overwhelmed, being a victim, competing and losing, being hopeless or desperate, going in debt, making promises you cannot keep, etc.

PART 3: Do this part with a partner, or if you can arrange it, in a group of 3.

1) One person is the first Client. The others are Possibilitators.

2) Client reads their list from PART 1 out loud about how they block their Being from showing up.

3) Titles a new page in their Beep! Book: EXPERIMENTS FOR SHOWING UP.

4) Asks, "Would you please give me Possibility about ways to unblock my Being from showing up?

5) And then for 8 minutes write as fast as you can what the Possibilitators say. Not what you think they say or understand them to say, but what they actually say.

6) When you are finished, ask them this question: Will you give me possibilties on ways to unblock my Being from evolving?" 

7) If they say, "Yes," then Title a new page in your Beep! Book: EXPERIMENTS FOR EVOLVING. Then for 8 minutes write down their suggestions as fast as you can.

8) Then change roles and do the same for the other two, one at a time.

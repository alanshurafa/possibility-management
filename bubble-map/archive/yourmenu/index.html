Your Menu on Strikingly

Another great compilation from the last 4 street experiments to offer our Non-Material Value made by Claire Daniel Thompson, Nikki Wagner, and Devin Gleeson.

It all started in Denmark at the Global Ecovillage Network gathering July 2022, in a conversation with Anne-Chloé Destremau (who has an astounding propensity for incendiary ideas...), who was pulling vivid descriptions of Archiarchy out of her Archetypal Lineage.

Her words, “If only there were shopping malls but instead of things to buy, it would be full of Non-Material Value...” hit my 5 Bodies, and that experience became clearly imprinted in me as a market of Archiarchal Value.

Later in the Village Lab in Poland, that vision came to life even more with the Intimacy Café. The idea was so incendiary that in the two online Gremlin Transformation groups, everyone was ready to publicly Take A Stand loudly in a Café about their Nonaterial Value.

A few months later we had a Team call. Back then the planned Seattle ETB was still on (it has since been cancelled), and we had no registrations. Devin Gleeson asked for possibilities to reach out to people and get himself ‘out there’. After sharing his fear of showing himself, Nicole and I showered Devin with so much clear and High Level Fun Possibility that he came out of the call with a full tank of energy to practice.

One of the most exciting Experiments Devin decided to try was to head to the streets, and publicly set up ‘shop’, meaning to create a Non-Material Value space on the street, with a Menu of offers that the Spaceholder would deliver. We were so juiced up, that Nicole booked her flights from Miami, so she could get to Boulder and do this experiment in a Team.

The results are beyond successful!

Delivering Your Menu allows you to practice so many Possibilitator Skills: Shift Identity, make Authentic contact, Become Centered, Grounded, Bubbled, become Unhookable, Cavitate Space, Hold Space, Navigate space, Go Nonlinear, Make Legends, and reveal the client’s own Nonmaterial Value to themselves. If anyone would like to experience High Level Fun, then try it out with at least one buddy!

Hints that worked that we learned:

  • Make contact. Say hello and look at people in the eyes;
  • Prepare what your offers are in advance. Your Box might be preventing you from actually delivering the value by keeping you busy getting chairs, or writing out the Menu Board, and in the meantime the sun goes down.
  • Do NOT adapt to the people's Context. Do not make this experience of sitting in the chair across from you nice, or easy, or understandable to make it more comfortable for the Client's Box. This is also not about being particularly weird. It is about keeping the Clarity of the Context, while NOT speaking in the language of the Box of the people passing by.
  • When approaching people to invite them to YOUR space, speak from Culture To Culture, where your culture is not better, and their culture is not better.
  • Be Radically Honest while Radically Relating.
  • When a Gremlin comes to sit in Your space with an unconscious Intention to sabotage it, immediately put the 'Poop' On The Table by clearly and precisely describing the Gremlin strategies at play. Explain that this Menu space you are holding is not for their Gremlin to eat. It is for the Adult Part interested in 3 Phase Healing and Evolution.

    NO ONE CAN DO IT FOR YOU

    What if you want something different from football, the bar, deer hunting, the army, gambling, the church, or corporate profits?

    What do you do about this? Do you hope that someone magically calls you up and offers you the perfect Team? Stay alone and keep waiting? Join the existing Teams anyway and try to convince yourself that they are satisfying?

    Your unfulfilled desires for exploring, practicing new skills, discovery, transforming emotional pains, edgework, vitality, creative collaboration, adventure, evolution, intimacy experiments, etc., may remain unfulfilled until you call your own Team together.

    Why is that? Because you are the one with those exact desires.

    But why do YOU have to do it? This is not fair!

    Why does it have to be YOU? The answer to this question is simple: Because you are the one who can.

    If you do not call your Team together, this Team cannot come together.

    Have you been waiting around for someone else to discover your secret desires and talents and call you to be a part of a Team exactly like you want to be a part of?

    That's sadly funny, isn't it? How are 'they' supposed to know what kind of Team you long to be part of? Are 'they' supposed to read your mind, heart, and Being?

    How many years have gone by already? Are you going to keep waiting?

    If you do not call your Team together, probably it won't happen... ever.

    What do you choose?

    HOW TO CALL YOUR TEAMS TOGETHER

    Many skills are needed to successfully originate a Team, for example, Centering, Spaceholding, Communicating, Transforming Reactivity through Emotional Healing Processes, Navigating 3 Worlds, Legend Making, Going Nonlinear, Going Unreasonable, Gremlin Hunting, etc. (See the LIST OF SUGGESTIONS below.)

    In fact, learning, practicing, and deepening these skills can become a central resource of Matrix-Building nurturance that feeds your Team.

    One lesson we learned about creating and navigating your Team is that, no matter how much you 'know' in theory, authentic Team skills are learned in practice. That means, the sooner you start your Team, the sooner you learn how to start your Team.

    Another thing we learned is that you will never get it perfect. There are always new surprises emerging as you go along, new frontiers to explore, new capacities to develop, in yourself, in the others, and in your Team as a living organism.

    So forget about figuring it all out before you start, or getting it perfect ever. Your Team will roll along, growing, diversifying, deepening, transforming, perhaps even dying. And it will entertain, challenge, and feed you all the way.

    Calling your Team together in the first place includes a few basics. Building out this framework is like preparing your garden soil for seeds.

    BUILD THE BONES THAT YOUR TEAMS CAN GROW ON

    Start your Team with 3 people. You do not need more.

    If you call your friends, and say, "Hey, we are starting a cool meeting. You should all come over!" and you suddenly have 20 friends sitting around trying to make a Team they are inspired about, you end up with a mixed-context group.

    Get together with 2 other people and talk seriously about what each of you individually wants. A group cannot want anything! Only individuals can 'want'. Try to not have 'consensus'. Instead, try to have communication. Help each other navigate to undefendedness.

    Have a Metaconversation by asking piercing questions about what each is up to in their lives.

    As you talk, discover and lay down the bones of your Team. You are Gameworld Architects.

    You are building your regenerative-culture Village that meets for mutual benefit.

    Your work together changes the Morphogenetic Field of the human race.

    Each of your Team meetings evolves the context and content of the Global Ethnosphere.

    It is worth paying attention to what happens during your Team meetings, because the Space you Hold is one of the cutting edges of the evolution of human consciousness on Earth.

    For real.

    What follows here is a series of questions to answer specifically together with your Team Spaceholders. Take you time to answer. Several Team meetings is not too large an investment to build a solid foundation for your endeavor.

    Each participant will go through their own personal Liquid States on this journey to find answers that build out an exciting future. As the 3 of you continue to come together and deepen the context of your Team, other people will feel the waves that you generate in the field of influence for the emergence of next culture and they will be attracted.

    LIQUID STATES

    Learning big or truly new things changes the shape of your Being. You become a new person, a person who now is and knows and can create what you just a few moments before could neither be, know, or create.

    The in between condition of leaving behind one shape and not yet becoming a new shape is fluidic. It is the experience of bits and pieces of your various identities re-arranging their relationship and position with each other, and making new inner space for distinctions and insights that had no space in your previous shape. This inner re-arranging state we refer to as a 'Liquid State'.

    Since you have 5 Bodies (physical, intellectual, emotional, energetic, and archetypal), authentic learning often involves Liquid States in any (or all...) of your 5 Bodies, each with its unique form of groundlessness. In particular, you will feel both repressed and new feelings and emotions. There is a universe of difference between Feelings and Emotions.

    If you do not have the Thoughtmaps, Distinctions, and Clarity about, for example, the Feelings and Emotions emerging in your experience as you enter a Rapid Learning 5 Body Liquid State, you may falsely assume that you have a brain tumor or are going insane. Yes the experiences are intense. No there is nothing wrong with you. This is an actual Liquid State.

    Therefore, if your Team is dedicated to any kind of change, or if it functions in the service of a Bright Principle such as Learning, Exploration, Transformation, Evolution, Change, Discovery, Initiation, Growing Up, Development, Expansion, Inner Permaculture, Unfolding Potential, or even High Level Fun or Living Full Out, it would be extremely wise to skill everyone up in the arts of surfing Liquid States together, and how to Hold Space for others while they are going through Liquid States.

    Because, if there is no Liquid State, there is no change.

    This best source we know of for you and your Team to learn about Liquid States is available for free online at: http://liquidstate.mystrikingly.com.

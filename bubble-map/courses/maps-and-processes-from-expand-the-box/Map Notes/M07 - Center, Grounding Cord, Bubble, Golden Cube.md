# M07 — Center, Grounding Cord, Bubble, Golden Cube

*The four energetic-body practices, used together — the equipment that lets the learner stay present while thoughtware is loosening.*

![Being Adaptive and Being Centered — Possibility Management map](../Maps/M07.png)

**What it is.** Four energetic-body practices, used together. The operating posture of a PM learner at any moment of intensity is *centered, grounded, bubbled, with the cube open if work is being done.* **Center** is the energetic location in the body (lower belly / hara) from which choices, will, and authority come. **Grounding cord** is an energetic line declared from center to the centre of the earth — for discharge and stability. **Bubble** is an arm's-length sphere that clarifies what is yours and what is not. **Golden cube** is a portable, declared creative workspace for constructing and testing new thoughtware. Without the equipment, liquid state is destabilising; with it, liquid state is workable. The four compound — by the third or fourth pass they are yours in seconds.

**At a glance.** Center vs head → off-center, your authority sits in the other person's reaction or your phone · Find your center, don't pick it → hara for most, heart for some · Declared, not visualised → they exist because you said so · Giving your center away → handing authority to whoever seems to have it · Bubble as DISTINCTION, not PROTECTION → it clarifies, it does not block · Golden cube as workspace, not refuge → opened to build or test, closed when done.

## How to explain it verbally

These four are the equipment that lets you stay present while your thoughtware is loosening. Center is the place in your lower belly where your choices and authority come from; off-center, your authority sits in someone else's reaction or your phone. The grounding cord is a line you declare from center into the earth, for discharge and stability. The bubble is an arm's-length sphere that clarifies what is yours and what is not. The golden cube is a portable workspace you open to build or test new thoughtware. They are declared, not visualized: they exist because you said so. Without them a liquid state destabilizes; with them it becomes workable.

**If you only remember one thing:** the bubble is a distinction, not a shield, and all four exist because you declare them, not because you picture them.

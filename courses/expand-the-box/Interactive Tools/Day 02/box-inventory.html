The Box is the survival-and-protection thoughtware you assembled in childhood. It kept you alive then. You cannot tell it from yourself unless you step back far enough to look at it. Click a layer. Look at it. Name what's there. The Box is not on trial — it is being seen.

Disidentification grammar As you write each item, the verb is the work: I have this. I am not this. Surface items are habits you have, not selves you are. The small distance between am and have is the doorway the rest of this course walks through.

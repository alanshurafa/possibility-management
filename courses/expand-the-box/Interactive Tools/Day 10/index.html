The closing tool
I.
Composed ceremony · ~25 min · Three Powers sequenced

The Declaration Composer.

One declaration. Sourced from a bright principle. For the 90 days after the course.
A scaffolded walk through the Three Powers — own the choice (Power I), ask a dangerous question (Power II), find the bright principle the declaration will source from (M21), compose the declaration (Power III), then speak it aloud three times in a ceremonial moment. The final card is printable. On return visits the tool surfaces your prior declaration and offers to re-speak it.
~25 min · standing for the final step Begin →
· · ·

Why only one tool today.

Day 10 is integration. The work is not to add more material — it is to consolidate what the nine prior modules already installed and to make the move that carries it forward. One declaration, made cleanly, sourced from a bright principle, sized to your actual life.

The tool does not produce the declaration for you. It scaffolds the sequence — choose, ask, source, declare — so that the declaration you leave with is one your body can hold past tomorrow morning.

What this tool saves.

Your declaration, the dangerous question you sat with, the bright principles it is sourced from, the date you made it, and the horizon date 90 days out — all stored in your browser, on this device only. Nothing is sent anywhere.

Print the card. Put it where you will see it for the 90 days that follow. Come back here whenever you want to re-speak it. A declaration creates a context; living inside the context is the work that comes next.

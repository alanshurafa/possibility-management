# M17 — Ego States

*Five distinct locations from which you speak, feel and choose — Parent, Child, Adult, Gremlin, Demon — and the lens that lets you see, in real time, which one is on the line.*

![Map of Ego-States — Possibility Management map](../Maps/M17.png)

**What it is.** Five distinct locations from which a human speaks, feels and chooses: Parent, Child, Adult, Gremlin, Demon. Not personality types, not developmental stages — *states* you move in and out of, sometimes within a single sentence. Related to the Parent-Child-Adult model from Transactional Analysis but not the same map: TA has no Gremlin or Demon, and PM names both on purpose so the learner does not mistake gremlin food for adult choice or shadow material for personality. The Adult is the location from which PM is actually practiced; the others are visited.

**At a glance.** Parent → Critical (judges) / Nurturing (takes care); internalized authority · Child → Free (spontaneous, alive) / Adapted (performs, complies to survive) · Adult → present-time, holds distinctions, chooses on purpose; PM's operating location · Gremlin → wants drama and suffering, speaks sotto voce, passes for thought · Demon → the harm-capable part; not a metaphor, not a sacred shadow; a known Demon is far safer than an unknown one. States, not layers — Adult one second, Adapted Child the next, nothing visible changing. (Active Demon work belongs in a trainer container or with a trauma-qualified clinician — this map only locates it.)

## How to explain it verbally

Ego states are five locations you speak, feel, and choose from: Parent, Child, Adult, Gremlin, and Demon. They are not personality types or stages. They are states you move in and out of, sometimes inside one sentence. Parent is your internalized authority. Child is free or adapted and performing. Adult is present-time, holds distinctions, chooses on purpose, and is where PM is practiced. It overlaps Transactional Analysis, but PM adds the Gremlin, which wants drama, and the Demon, the part with real capacity for harm. PM names both on purpose, because a known Demon is far safer than an unknown one. Active Demon work belongs in a trainer container.

**If you only remember one thing:** these are states you visit, not types you are, and the Adult is the one you practice from.

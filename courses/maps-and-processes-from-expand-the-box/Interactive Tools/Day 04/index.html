The tool
I.
Form-driven designer · printable card

Experiment Designer.

Pick a domain. Pick a starter from the Experiment Bank — or write your own. Write what / by when / what I will notice. Print the card. Run it. Notice.
The PM experiment structure is three lines: what / by when / what I will notice. If any is missing, it is not yet an experiment — it is a wish. This tool scaffolds the three lines, gives you 50+ starter templates pulled from the Experiment Bank, and tracks the rep through PENDING → RUNNING → COMPLETED (or ABANDONED — that's data too).
~10 min design · ongoing rep Open →
· · ·

How to use this.

Read the Day 4 module first (`Days/Day 04 - Feedback, Coaching, Rapid Learning, Experiments.md`). Then open the Designer and write your first experiment. Print the card. Carry it. Run the rep.

Then come back. Mark it RUNNING when you start. Mark it COMPLETED when you finish — and write what you actually noticed in the field that appears. That captured noticing is the Beep. It feeds the next Shift, which feeds the next Go.

One experiment at a time. Two in parallel — neither runs cleanly.

What the tool saves.

Your experiments, their status, and what you noticed afterward — stored in your browser's local storage on this device only. Never sent anywhere.

Clear your browser data and the tool forgets. That is the design. Your Beep! Book — the literal notebook — is still the engineering log. This is a companion, not a replacement.

Reps compound. One experiment doesn't transform you. Thirty in a row do.

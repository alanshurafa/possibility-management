Feeling Locator.

Sit with your body. Locate the sensation. Identify which of the four feelings it is. Receive the action it asks for.
A guided embodied practice. Click the body zones where you feel something. Name the quality. The tool suggests which of the four feelings the sensation most resembles — anger, sadness, fear, joy — and shows you the asked-for action. End with a three-line voice message to your partner.

What this tool is.

Day 5 introduces three maps: the Old Map of Feelings (M08), the Numbness Bar (M09), and the New Map of Feelings (M10). The Feeling Locator is the interactive companion to doing what those maps describe — registering a feeling in your body, in real time, at low intensity.

Feelings are minutes long. Emotions are years long. The Numbness Bar is what kept you from feeling this until now. If the feeling you locate turns out to be mixed — anger and sadness, fear and joy — that's an emotion, not a feeling. Day 6 is where mixed emotions get unmixed.

How to use it.

Read the Day 5 module first (`Days/Day 05 - Feelings vs Emotions, Old Map of Feelings, Numbness Bar.md`). Confirm your partner is reachable. Confirm you have 30+ minutes. Then come here.

Run the tool slowly. The point is not insight — the point is the rep. Each rep lowers the Numbness Bar a notch. Your sessions save locally on this device. Nothing leaves your browser.

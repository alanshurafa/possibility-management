How this fits the module

What this tool is.

Day 8 is tagged Medium intensity not because the material is conceptually hard but because doing this on your actual material lands closer to the bone than reading any map. The builder is the bridge — you have the framework in your head; the builder helps you walk it into a real, specific, plannable rep.

Use it after you have read the Day 8 module and the M16 Map Note. Use it before the partner exchange if you want to bring something ready. Use it for the between-module experiment — one real completion loop in 48 hours.

What it saves.

Every loop you build is stored in your browser's local storage on this device only — never sent anywhere. Drafts are autosaved while you work. The loop card prints clean for carrying.

Old conversations can be loop-completed years later. The energetic debt is patient — and so is the resolution. The builder treats every loop the same: small recent items, long-overdue items, retroactive completions from a decade ago.

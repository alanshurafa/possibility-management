Embodied lens · 5 states · ~25 min High intensity

Ego State Locator.

Type one sentence you actually said. Locate which of the five states it came from. Then ask: what would Adult say?
Parent · Adult · Child · Gremlin · Demon. Locations, not personality types. You move in and out of all five — sometimes within a single sentence. The tool gives you the linguistic markers and the body cues for each, then asks you to be honest about where the sentence actually sat. The Adult is the rarest of the five. Most learners think they're in Adult when they're in Critical Parent or Gremlin.

How to use this.

Read the Day 9 module first (Days/Day 09 - Ego States, Problem Ownership, Learning Spiral.md) and watch the three videos. The tool is the embodied companion — the place you practice the lens with one of your own sentences.

Open the locator after the videos, not before. Use a sentence from the last 24 hours, not one you invent for the tool. Honest answers are usually surprising. Save the catch. Come back next week with another.

After twenty reps, the pattern will be visible from the outside — including the state you most often claim to be in but aren't.

What the tool saves.

Each saved catch — sentence, identified state, Adult rephrase — stays in your browser's local storage on this device only. Never sent anywhere.

Clear your browser data and the tool forgets. That is the design. The log is your practice record, not the course's.

The Demon section is deliberately bounded. Naming Demon is the work this tool supports — nothing further. Demon work that goes deeper requires a PM trainer or a trauma-qualified clinician; the GT Gremlin Transformation training is the longer container.

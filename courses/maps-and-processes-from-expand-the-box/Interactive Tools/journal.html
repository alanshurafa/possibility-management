Every tool in this suite saves to your browser, and only to your browser. This page gathers all of it in one place — what you have done, what you wrote, when — and lets you save it to a file you own.

Privacy · read this once Everything on this page is stored only in this browser, on this device. Nothing is sent anywhere — no server, no account, no telemetry.

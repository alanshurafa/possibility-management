# M27 — Your Gremlin

*The King or Queen of your Underworld — the part of you that wants drama, suffering and righteousness as food, and runs your life from below your awareness until you meet it on purpose.*

![Your Gremlin — Possibility Management map](../Maps/M27.png)

**What it is.** A hand-drawn portrait, not a diagram — the Gremlin drawn as a resident so you stop treating it as an abstraction. It is the part of you that wants **gremlin food**: drama, suffering, righteousness, being right, being a victim, being special, distraction. Sovereign below your awareness — the King or Queen of your Underworld — and the map states the cost plainly: until you meet it consciously, *it owns your life.* It is distinct from Being and from the Box (the Box wants safety; the Gremlin wants food), it speaks in a voice that feels true, and it eats *"I'm the worst"* and *"I'm the best"* with equal appetite.

**At a glance.** Wants food, not safety (the cut from the Box) · Distinct from Being and Box — *you have it, you are not it* · Speaks *sotto voce* in a voice that passes for insight · On a low-drama feast right now (M13): the sigh, the eye-roll, the rehearsed righteousness · Eats both directions — grandiosity and self-disgust are the same meal · Not the enemy, cannot be killed — meet it, know it, befriend it, put it to use. **Scope:** meeting and naming your Gremlin is in scope here; deep Gremlin process work (Gremlin Transformation) is a separate container held by a PM trainer / the GT training — same discipline as the Demon scope note in M17. Do not attempt to "transform" it solo.

## How to explain it verbally

Your Gremlin is the King or Queen of your Underworld, drawn as a portrait, not a diagram, so you stop treating it as an abstraction. It is the part of you that wants gremlin food: drama, suffering, righteousness, being right, being the victim, being special. It is sovereign below your awareness, and the map says the cost plainly: until you meet it on purpose, it owns your life. It is not the Box; the Box wants safety, the Gremlin wants food. It eats *I am the worst* and *I am the best* with the same appetite. You cannot kill it; you meet it, befriend it, and put it to work.

**If you only remember one thing:** you have a Gremlin and it is hungry, not evil; meet it on purpose or it runs your life unmet.

    Possibility Management
    Possibility Management Polska

    Possibility Management is a global Gameworld providing: Healing, Adulthood Initiations, Thoughtware Upgrades, Transformational Skills, and Personal Agency for creating the culture you would love to live in.

    The first Expand The Box Training, delivered in March 1998 by Clinton Callahan, marked the start of Possibility Management.

    In the beginning, Possibility Management was organized as a personal developement program. In 2007, Possibilitators realized that they were uncovering something much greater than useful tools for better relationships: they were inventing thoughtware for next culture, Archiarchy!

    Since then, Possibility Management has expanded, transformed and deepened in unpredictable dimensions through the radical and authentic research of Possibilitators around the world.

    The core Trainings for Authentic Adulthood and Archetypal Initiations are Expand The Box Training and Possibility Labs.

    In 2025, for the fourth consecutive year, Anne-Chloé Destremau and Clinton Callahan are back in Poland (near Warsaw) to deliver 3 back-to-back Trainings: an Expand The Box, a Possibility Lab and a Heal From School Lab.

  • Tuition & Discount

    Discount for the Trainings in Poland 2025 -

    If you participate in all 3 trainings, there is a 13% discount on the total of the base price (lowest part of the sliding scale) of your country of residence. The 13% discount does not apply to the accomodation and food, which are paid separately from the tuition.

    Tuition -

    For Expand The Box Trainings (ONLY): Each time you participate in an ETB, the tuition lowers. Here's how --

    For full-time residents in various countries we use a Tier Pricing Plan:

    1. RED TIER - Countries such as Germany, France, UK. The tuition for the first Expand The Box is a sliding scale 650-950 €. The tuition investment for the second ETB is 400 €, third ETB is 200 €, fourth ETB 100 €, from fifth ETB on, 0 €.
    2. NOMAD TIER - For nomadic participants, no matter your country of birth or last residence, the tuition for the first Expand The Box is a sliding scale between 550-950 €. The tuition investment for the second ETB is 350 €, third ETB is 175 €, fourth ETB 90 €, from fifth ETB on, 0 €.
    3. BLUE TIER - Countries such as Portugal, Italy, and Poland. The tuition for the first Expand The Box is a sliding scale 450-950 €. The tuition investment for the second ETB is 300 €, third ETB is 150 €, fourth ETB is 75 €, from fifth ETB on, 0 €.
    4. YELLOW TIER - Countries such as Brazil, Mexico, and other Global South countries. The investment for the first Expand The Box is a sliding scale 265-950 €. The tuition investment for the second ETB is 150 €, third ETB is 75 €, fourth ETB is 30 €, from fifth ETB on, 0 €.

    The tuition for the Possibility Lab and the Heal From School Lab is as such, for full-time residents in:

    1. RED TIER - Countries such as Germany, France, USA, UK. The tuition for Possibility Labs is a sliding scale 650-950 €.
    2. NOMAD TIER - No matter your country of birth or last residence, the tuition for Possibility Labs is a sliding scale between 550-950 €.
    3. BLUE TIER - Countries such as Portugal, Italy, and Poland. The tuition for Possibility Labs is a sliding scale 450-950 €.
    4. YELLOW TIER - Countries such as Brazil, Mexico, and other Global South countries. The tuition for Possibility Labs is a sliding scale 265-950 €.

  • Logistics

    Location

    The Expand The Box, Possibility Lab and Heal From School Lab are taking place in the same location: Dworku pod Wiechą.

    Dworek is situated 30 minutes from Chopin Airport (Warsaw), and 45 minutes from downtown Warsaw.

    https://dworekpodwiecha.pl/

    https://maps.app.goo.gl/smho5YZE14sm4PVJ8

    Getting There

    The logistics letter that will be send to you 5 to 6 weeks before the beginning of the training will give you extended public and private transport options to make your way to Dworek.

    Ubers and taxis will easily take you there.

    We will also open a Telegram group (link in the logistics letter) so you can organize among yourselves shared cars.

    Accomodation

    You will sleep in single beds in shared rooms of 3 to 6 people. Each room has its own bathroom.

    As the trainings take place during the summer, you can also sleep in tent on the property. We do not provide any camping equipement, bring your own.

    The price of accomodation is dividing among the number of participants. The final price will be announced during the trainings.

    From past years, the cost of accomodation for 5 days is around 150 € / 600 zl.

    Food

    Part of the training skill-building is preparing our meals together. Food will be bought beforehand by the organization team, no need to bring your own meals.

    There will be 3 meals a day, plus snacks on breaks during the ETB only.

    The cost of food is divided among ourselves. The final price will be announced during each training.

    From past years' experience, the cost of food for 5 days is around 60€/250zl for the ETB, and 50€/215zl for Labs.

  • Trainings

    Four back-to-back trainings from 13 to 14 August, 2025

    Expand The Box

    13-17 August 2025

    Expand The Box is the core training for Possibility Management: a safe and astonishing learning environment for upgrading traditional thinking and behaviors.

    You will access new possibilities, new perspectives, and new skills for creating a life full of vividness and authenticity.

    Without your knowing how, the standard thinking and behavior patterns you adopted from your parents, your culture, and your education system could severely limit both the quality of your relationships and your ability to respond creatively to the opportunities and challenges of life.

    In Expand the Box you will explore new thought­maps and practice new forms of experiencing. You will train individually, in pairs, in small groups and as a whole group to enter new territory and discover hidden fields of human possibility. We will create the safest of conditions, where you can make maximum mistakes with only positive consequences.

    More about Expand The Box at: http://expandthebox.mystrikingly.com

    Possibility Village Lab

    20-24 August 2025

    Completing Expand The Box qualifies you to attend the Possibility Labs. In Possibility Labs you change your relationship to inner blocks by getting deep insights into your true needs and hidden purposes.

    Through approaching your essential inspiration and applying practical tools and techniques of Possibility Management, you pave the way for reviving your true vocation, no matter how big your vocation is.

    Possibility Labs provide a safe space for your ongoing initiation into responsible adulthood and into a purpose-driven life.

    More about the Possibility Village Lab at: http://possibilitylabs.mystrikingly.com

    Heal From School Lab

    27-31 August 2025

    You carry wounds from being in public school, ranging from being adaptive, disavowing your own authority, being terrified of being creative, and holding on to negative assessments about your value from testing and teacher's comments as voices in your head. These impressions and experiences stay in you for the rest of your life. They shape what is possible for you until you do the adult and archetypal healing and initiatory processes that change your wounds into gateways.

    What most people learn in school is that they hate learning, cannot write what they think about, hate reading books, despise math, and fail on purpose because when you win, it means others must lose.

    School is a test environment for learning to compete and survive the rest of your life. Being oriented towards competing to survive blocks you from finding and developing your true talents and gifts, because most of your gifts are not mentioned in the curriculum. If you love something not taught at school, they imply you are crazy or dangerous.

    Public school was invented 150 years ago to prepare workers to endure life as a production worker and to prepare soldiers to obey orders from 'superiors'. To send a child to school in the 21st Century is physical, emotional, intellectual, and psychological abuse.

    Each day you spent in school was one less day you had to unfold your special potentials for getting prepared for what is coming.

    Through specific, strong, transformational, healing and adulthood initiatory processes, Heal From School Lab is part of igniting your adulthood path in Life. Plus, during this Lab, we practice basic and advanced skills for authentic creation, radical relating, and going into discovery together.

    “The plain fact is that the planet does not need more 'successful' people. But it does desperately need more peacemakers, healers, restorers, storytellers, and lovers of every kind. It needs people who live well in their places. It needs people of moral courage willing to join the fight to make human society regenerative and humane. These qualities have little to do with success as modern culture has defined it.”

    ― David W. Orr

    Participating in an Expand The Box Training is a requirement to join the Heal From School Lab. We highly recommend that you also participate in a Rage Club (https://rageclub.org) (online or offline) as serious preparation for engaging with this Lab.

    More about the Heal From School at: https://healfromschool.mystrikingly.com/
